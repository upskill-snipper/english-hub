# EXAMINER ADVISORY AGREEMENT

> **TEMPLATE — NOT FOR EXECUTION**
> This is a drafting template. It must be reviewed by a qualified English lawyer before signature.

**Use this agreement when:** the examiner is acting as an **advisor only** — lending their name, sitting on an advisory board, taking occasional calls about curriculum or strategy, or commenting on content at a high level. They are **not** writing or editing substantive content.

**If the advisor starts writing content**, swap to the contributor agreement.

## BACKGROUND

(A) The Company is building an online English learning platform and maintains an advisory board of subject-matter experts.

(B) The Advisor holds, or has held, appointments as an examiner, assessor, moderator or team leader with one or more UK Awarding Organisations (the **"Credentials"**, detailed in Schedule 1).

(C) The Advisor has agreed to act as an advisor to the Company and to permit the Company to describe the Advisor's involvement in the Company's marketing, on the terms of this Agreement.

---

## AGREED TERMS

### 1. Services

1.1 The Advisor shall, for such reasonable time as the Parties agree (and not exceeding **[X] hours per quarter** without further agreement), provide the Company with advisory services which may include:

 (a) attendance at quarterly advisory board meetings;

 (b) ad hoc calls with the Company's leadership on curriculum direction, specification changes and market positioning;

 (c) high-level review and commentary on the Company's product and content strategy;

 (d) introductions to relevant industry contacts.

1.2 The Advisor is **not** required under this Agreement to write, author, edit or review any instructional content. If the Parties wish the Advisor to take on such work, they shall enter a separate Examiner Contributor Agreement for that purpose.

1.3 The Advisor shall provide the services with reasonable care and skill and in accordance with professional standards.

### 2. Status

2.1 The Advisor is engaged as an **independent contractor**. Nothing in this Agreement creates a contract of employment, agency, partnership or joint venture.

2.2 The Advisor is responsible for their own taxes arising from any Fees.

### 3. Intellectual property in advisory outputs

3.1 The Advisor may, in the course of providing services under this Agreement, produce notes, slides, written feedback, emails or other incidental materials (**"Advisory Outputs"**).

3.2 The Advisor hereby assigns to the Company, with full title guarantee, the entire right, title and interest (including all Intellectual Property Rights) in and to the Advisory Outputs, for their full term throughout the world.

3.3 The Advisor irrevocably waives all moral rights in the Advisory Outputs in favour of the Company and its successors and assigns.

3.4 Background IP owned by the Advisor remains the Advisor's property. If Background IP is incorporated into Advisory Outputs, the Advisor grants the Company a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, sub-licensable and transferable licence to use it as part of those outputs.

### 4. Marketing use of name and biography

4.1 **Grant.** The Advisor grants the Company a **perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, sub-licensable and transferable** right and licence to use the Advisor's name, photograph, biographical information and Credentials in marketing and promotional materials for the Company's business.

4.2 **Permitted statements.** The Company may state truthfully that the Advisor serves (or has served) as an advisor to the Company, describe the Advisor's examiner credentials in Schedule 1, and use descriptive phrases such as *"advised by former Edexcel examiner [Name]"*, *"with guidance from examiner [Name]"* or similar.

4.3 **Disclaimer.** The Company shall not imply that the Advisor's role as an examiner for any Awarding Organisation constitutes an endorsement, approval or accreditation by that Awarding Organisation of the Company or its products. The Company shall include a disclaimer to this effect where reasonably practicable.

4.4 **No derogatory use.** The Company shall not use the Advisor's name, image or biography in a manner that is derogatory, defamatory or likely to cause reputational harm to the Advisor.

4.5 **Survival.** The rights granted under this clause 4 survive termination of this Agreement.

### 5. Warranties

5.1 The Advisor warrants that:

 (a) the Credentials set out in Schedule 1 are accurate and not misleading;

 (b) the Advisor is not subject to any obligation to any Awarding Organisation or third party that prevents the Advisor from entering into this Agreement; and

 (c) in providing services the Advisor will not disclose to the Company any **Exam Board Confidential Material** (including but not limited to unreleased papers, unpublished mark schemes, candidate scripts, confidential meeting minutes or any material marked as confidential by an Awarding Organisation).

5.2 If the Advisor becomes aware that they have inadvertently disclosed Exam Board Confidential Material, they shall notify the Company immediately.

### 6. Fees

6.1 The Advisor shall receive the fees set out in **Schedule 2**. These may take the form of a retainer, a per-meeting attendance fee, equity in the Company under a separate option agreement, or any combination.

6.2 The Advisor shall invoice the Company monthly or quarterly as agreed. The Company shall pay undisputed invoices within 30 days of receipt.

### 7. Confidentiality

7.1 The Advisor shall keep confidential any Confidential Information of the Company disclosed in the course of the engagement, shall not disclose it to any third party, and shall use it only for the purposes of this Agreement.

7.2 This obligation survives termination for **five (5) years**.

### 8. Non-solicit (light)

8.1 During the Term and for **six (6) months** after termination, the Advisor shall not directly solicit, entice away, or endeavour to entice away any employee or contributor of the Company with whom the Advisor had material contact during the engagement.

8.2 There is no non-compete restriction in this Agreement. The Advisor is free to advise or work with any other organisation, subject to the confidentiality obligations in clause 7.

### 9. Term and termination

9.1 This Agreement commences on the date above and continues until terminated by either Party on **thirty (30) days'** written notice.

9.2 Either Party may terminate immediately on written notice for material breach or insolvency.

9.3 On termination, clauses 3 (IP), 4 (marketing use), 5 (warranties), 7 (confidentiality), 8 (non-solicit) and 10 (general) survive.

### 10. General

10.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of **England and Wales** and the Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

10.2 Notices must be in writing and may be sent by post or e-mail.

10.3 No variation is effective unless in writing signed by both Parties.

10.4 A person who is not a Party has no rights under the Contracts (Rights of Third Parties) Act 1999.

10.5 Electronic signatures are accepted. This Agreement may be executed in counterparts.
