Sub-processor Register

The single source of truth — every third party that may process personal data, what they do, where, and the status of their data-processing agreement.

Reconciled against the codebase 2026-05-17 · status shown honestly

This register is the reconciled union of every provider referenced anywhere on the platform. The DPA-status column is deliberately honest: "pending" means a standard or clickwrap DPA exists and counter-signature is being formalised; "in finalisation" means the written agreement is being closed with counsel and is not yet evidenced. We do not upgrade a status without the paperwork in hand.

Honest note on the AI sub-processor

Anthropic is the only LLM provider the platform calls (code-verified — there is no OpenAI or other model integration). Our contractual position is that submissions are not used to train models, and we apply strong data-minimisation at the AI boundary (no directly identifying fields are sent). The counter-signed AI data-processing agreement and the written zero-retention confirmation are being finalised with counsel; until those are in hand we describe this as "in finalisation" rather than claiming more. We will share both with your DPO once closed.

For schools specifically

The consent-gated analytics and growth tools above are off by default for every under-18 account under the Children's Code high-privacy defaults. In a school deployment, the providers that touch student data are the infrastructure set (Supabase, Azure, Vercel, Cloudflare, Sentry, the email provider) plus the AI sub-processor (Anthropic) — all under data minimisation. Stripe is engaged only where an adult makes a payment; under-18 accounts cannot enter payment data.

Change notification

We notify controllers of any intended addition or replacement of a sub-processor before it goes live, giving the opportunity to object. The current live register is also published at theenglishhub.app/data-processing and is updated within 30 days of any material change.
