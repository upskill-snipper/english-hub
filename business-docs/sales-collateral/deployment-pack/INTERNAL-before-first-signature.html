1. Anthropic AI DPA + written no-training / zero-retention confirmation

The deployment pack (Docs 02, 03, 06, 07) states "no training on submissions" as our contractual position being finalised. Get the counter-signed Anthropic commercial DPA and a written ZDR / no-training confirmation in hand. Until then, do not upgrade the wording to "confirmed".

2. UK solicitor review of the Pilot Agreement (01) + DPA (02) templates

These are drafted templates, not lawyer-settled contracts. Get a UK solicitor to review both once — especially the liability cap, termination, and the controller/processor clauses — before a school's counsel marks them up. One review covers all future pilots.

Ref: Doc 01, Doc 02

3. Confirm the transactional email provider + get its DPA

The register lists the email provider as "Resend / Postmark — unconfirmed" because neither package is in package.json. Confirm which is actually live, then secure its DPA and fix Doc 03 + the public sub-processor list.

4. Counter-sign the core infrastructure DPAs

Supabase, Microsoft Azure, Vercel, Cloudflare, Stripe, Sentry are all "pending counter-signature". Execute the clickwrap/standard DPAs and keep copies on file so you can hand them to a DPO on request. These touch student data, so they're in scope before a school signs.

5. Reconcile the retention periods (live inconsistency)

The public Qatar privacy page §7 says account data is kept "Account duration + 3 years"; the RoPA and Doc 05 say "active + 24 months". Pick one, fix the other. A DPO who reads both will flag the contradiction.

Ref: /legal/privacy-qatar §7 vs RoPA v1 vs Doc 05
