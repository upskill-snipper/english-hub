How to use this

This is a short-form pilot agreement, deliberately one page of terms. It is provided as a template for signature. For a school's own legal review, our standard Data Processing Agreement (Document 02) sits alongside it. Both can be reviewed by your counsel before signature.

Success criteriaAs agreed in writing at the Week-1 kickoff and recorded in the kickoff note (Document 10). If the agreed criteria are not met, the Customer is under no obligation to proceed to annual deployment and owes nothing beyond the pilot fee.

3. What the Provider will do

  • Provide whole-cohort access to the platform for the pilot scope above, for the pilot term.
  • Deliver the Week-1 kickoff call, a Week 4–6 mid-pilot review, and a close-out review.
  • Provide founder-level support, responding within 24 working hours to issues raised by the project owner.
  • Process personal data only in accordance with the Data Processing Agreement (Document 02).
  • On request at any time, provide an exit pack within 48 hours: full data export, written deletion confirmation, and an audit certificate.

4. What the School will do

  • Nominate a project owner and provide a student roster for the pilot cohort.
  • Ensure it has a lawful basis to share student data with the Provider and that any required parental consents are in place (the platform's age-gate and parental-consent flow assist with this; see Documents 06 and 11).
  • Participate in the three pilot calls and provide feedback.
  • Pay the pilot fee within 30 days of invoice.

5. Data protection

The Provider acts as a data processor and the School as data controller in respect of student personal data processed for the pilot. Processing is governed by the Data Processing Agreement (Document 02), which forms part of this agreement. The Provider complies with the UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, the ICO Age Appropriate Design Code (Children's Code), and — for students resident in the State of Qatar — Law No. 13 of 2016 (PDPPL).

6. Term, termination and renewal

  • No auto-renew. This agreement ends at the close of the pilot term unless both parties sign a separate annual deployment agreement.
  • Termination at will. Either party may terminate during the pilot on 7 days' written notice. On termination the Provider refunds the pilot fee pro-rated to the weeks not yet delivered. There is no clawback or penalty.
  • On termination or expiry, the Provider deletes or returns student personal data per Document 05 and provides the exit pack on request.

7. Liability

Each party's total liability under this agreement is capped at the pilot fee. Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential loss. Nothing limits liability that cannot be limited by law.

8. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of England & Wales. The parties may agree to move to the laws of the State of Qatar for any subsequent annual deployment agreement if the School's procurement requires it.
