Student Roster Upload Guide

How to prepare and upload your pilot cohort — about 10 minutes.

Use the template file roster-template.csv in this pack

What we need (and what we don't)

The platform follows data-minimisation: we ask only for what is needed to create accounts and route students to the right content. Do not include home addresses, medical information, SEND flags, or any data beyond the columns below.

How to upload

  1. Open roster-template.csv from this pack in Excel or Google Sheets.
  2. Fill one row per student. Keep the header row exactly as provided.
  3. Save as CSV (UTF-8).
  4. At the Week-1 kickoff, we upload it together — or send it ahead via the secure link we provide (do not email a student roster as a plain attachment).
  5. Accounts activate automatically; under-16 accounts wait for the parental-consent loop to complete before going live.
Already have a Management Information System?

For annual deployments above ~500 students we can take a one-way export from iSAMS, Engage, Bromcom or Arbor instead of a manual CSV. For a pilot, the CSV is simplest and avoids an integration dependency.

Sending the roster securely

A student roster is personal data. Please don't email it as a plain attachment. We provide a secure upload link, or we upload it together on the kickoff screen-share. The file is processed under the DPA (Document 02) and retained per the schedule (Document 05).
