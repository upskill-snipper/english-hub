B. Data protection

Are you a controller or processor for school data?Processor; the school is the controller. DPA provided (Document 02).
Is a DPO appointed?Yes — Calum Johnson, dpo@theenglishhub.app (voluntary appointment; not mandatory under PDPPL)
Do you maintain a RoPA?Yes (UK GDPR Article 30) — available on request
Have you completed DPIAs?Yes — three: children's data, AI features, analytics
Lawful basis for processing student data?Contract (service delivery) + explicit consent (AI processing) + verifiable parental consent for under-13s
Where is data stored?Primary database in the EU (Supabase, Frankfurt); backend on Microsoft Azure UK South; AI inference in the US (Anthropic). Full topology in Document 06.
International transfer mechanism?Provider SCCs / UK addendum; for Qatar residents, explicit PDPPL Article 17 consent at sign-up naming each destination
Data-subject-rights response time?30 calendar days; self-service export / deletion / consent withdrawal in-product
Retention & deletion policy?Document 05; 48-hour exit pack on request

C. Children's data

Do you comply with the ICO Children's Code?Yes — high-privacy defaults enforced in code for all under-18s; strict test coverage ages 11–17
Minimum age?11. Ages 11–15 require a parent/guardian email; under-13s require verifiable parental consent (UK GDPR Art. 8)
Targeted advertising to minors?None. No behavioural profiling of minors for commercial purposes.
Can a parent see their child's essays?No — parents see progress summaries (modules, time, working-at grade), never essay text
Safeguarding process?Designated Safeguarding Lead appointed; disclosures in essay content routed to the DSL within 24h. Document 08.

D. AI

Which AI provider do you use?Anthropic (Claude) — the only LLM provider; code-verified. No OpenAI or other model.
What data is sent to the AI?Essay free-text, question text, exam board + year. No name, email, date of birth or school.
Is student data used to train AI models?No — our contractual position prohibits training on submissions. The counter-signed AI DPA + written zero-retention confirmation are being finalised with counsel (Document 03).
Is AI marking the final grade?No — AI marking is formative; teachers remain the final authority. Any AI-marked essay can be escalated to human review.
Is there human oversight?Yes — teacher override on every essay; safeguarding-relevant content routed to a human within 24h.

E. Information security

Encryption in transit / at rest?TLS 1.3 in transit; AES-256 at rest; Argon2id password hashing
Access control?Row-level security; least-privilege staff access with SSO + MFA; quarterly access review; audit logging
SSO for schools?Google Workspace / Microsoft 365 SSO supported on annual deployment
Penetration testing?Not yet independently pen-tested; on the security roadmap alongside ISO 27001
Certifications held?ICO registered. Not yet ISO 27001 / Cyber Essentials Plus / NIA certified (honest status; roadmap in Document 04)
Breach notification?Within 72 hours to the school; runbook + pre-filled regulator templates held
Cyber insurance?Available to confirm to procurement on request

Pricing?Pilot from £2,500 (year group) / £4,000 (department) / custom. Annual from £6,000 / £12,000 / custom from £25,000.
