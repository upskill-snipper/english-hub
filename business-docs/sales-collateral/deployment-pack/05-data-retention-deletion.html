Data Retention & Deletion Schedule

How long each category of data is kept, and what happens at the end of an engagement.

AI sub-processor side

Essay text sent to the AI sub-processor carries no directly identifying fields. Our contractual position is zero retention and no training on submissions; the written confirmation is being finalised with counsel (see Document 03). When closed, this row will state the confirmed AI-side retention explicitly.

Deletion on request

  • A student or parent can delete the account in one click from the privacy dashboard.
  • Deletion removes personal data within 30 days, with backup eviction completing within 90 days.
  • For children's accounts, deletion includes consent-ledger entries beyond the evidentiary minimum, on guardian request.

End of a school engagement — the exit pack

On termination or expiry of a pilot or annual deployment, at the school's choice we will either return or delete the school's student personal data. On request, we provide a 48-hour exit pack:

  • A full export of the school's student data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Written confirmation of deletion once the school confirms it has its export.
  • An audit certificate recording what was deleted and when.

Backups

Operational backups are retained on a rolling short window for disaster recovery and are evicted on the 90-day cycle above. Backups are encrypted at rest and are not used for any purpose other than recovery.
