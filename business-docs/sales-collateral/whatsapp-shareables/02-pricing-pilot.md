## The Founding Schools Programme

We are admitting the **first 10 schools** into the Founding Schools cohort at **£4,000 per school per year** — all-in, whole-school access for every student studying English from Year 7 through Year 13.

| Tier | Founding (now) | Standard (from August 2026) |
|---|---|---|
| Whole-school annual deployment | **£4,000 / year** | £8,000 / year |
| Cohort cap | **10 schools** | unlimited |

### What "for the life of the licence" means

The price you join at is the price you keep. When our standard pricing doubles to £8,000 in August 2026, Founding-cohort schools stay at £4,000 indefinitely — for as long as the school renews continuously, no annual increase, no surprise mid-contract uplift.

### Why we cap at 10

We are a small founder-led team. Ten Founding schools is the number we can support with **founder-level attention** — direct access to me, 24-working-hour response on anything raised by your project owner, bi-weekly platform updates with at least one improvement per cycle prioritising your requests. After ten, we move to standard support tiers and standard pricing.

## The pilot — try before the annual commitment

Before any annual deployment, we run a **one-term pilot** with defined success criteria agreed in writing before kickoff. **No auto-renew. No "minimum 3-year" lock-in.**

### Three pilot scopes — choose what fits

| Scope | Fee | Cohort | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A — Year-group pilot** | **from £2,500** | One year group (e.g. one Y11 IGCSE cohort, 60–120 students) | Schools wanting a low-risk test on the cohort that matters most this academic year |
| **B — Department pilot** | **from £4,000** | Whole English department, Y7–Y13 (300–800 students) | Schools committed to AI in the English department and wanting whole-cohort data from week 1 |
| **C — Multi-campus / group pilot** | **custom** | 2+ campuses | Education groups (Nord Anglia, ISP, GEMS, Cognita, local Doha chains) wanting a group-level proof-of-concept |

*Indicative founder pricing. Final pricing depends on school size, scope, and rollout requirements.*

**Pilot length:** ~one term (8–12 weeks), scoped to fit a natural half-term boundary at your school.

## Success criteria — agreed in writing in Week 1

We do not run open-ended evaluations. The criteria below are our defaults; we adapt them in the kickoff call to fit your school. **If we miss them, you do not renew and you owe nothing beyond the pilot fee.**

| Metric | Default target | How we measure |
|---|---|---|
| Department adoption | ≥ 70% of teachers active by Week 4 | Platform login data |
| Marking turnaround for the practice cohort | from ≥ 7 days → under 1 hour median | Platform analytics |
| Student time-on-platform per week | ≥ 25 minutes per active student | Platform analytics |
| Parent engagement with weekly emails | ≥ 40% open rate | Email analytics |
| Teacher NPS at close-out | ≥ +30 | Anonymous survey |

These are **defaults**. We change them in the kickoff call to fit what matters most to your department.

## What you commit to vs what we commit to

### What [Your School] commits to during the pilot

- **One project owner** (typically Head of English or Deputy Head Academic) — approximately 60 minutes / week of attention
- **Three 30-minute calls** — kickoff (Week 1), mid-review (Week 4–6), close-out (final week)
- **Honest feedback throughout** — what's working, what isn't, what to change
- A **student roster** for the pilot cohort (CSV upload, or use your MIS export)

That's it. No teacher training sessions to schedule. No "implementation consultant" charge. No integration project.

### What The English Hub commits to during the pilot

- **Founder-level support** within 24 working hours, direct
- **Bi-weekly platform updates** with at least one improvement per cycle prioritising your requests
- **Full PDPPL + UK GDPR + Children's Code posture** — Article 17 cross-border consent live in signup, three v1.0 DPIAs, separately-appointed DPO + DSL, EU-region primary database, zero AI-training on student data (contractual)

- **48-hour exit pack** at any point you ask for it: full data export, deletion confirmation, audit certificate
