Who needs to do what

Project owner
(Head of English / Deputy Head) Owns the pilot. ~60 min/week. Joins the 3 calls (kickoff, mid-review, close-out), uploads the student roster, briefs the department, gives honest feedback.
Data-protection lead
(DPO / IT) One 30-min call in Week 1. Reviews the compliance pack we send 24h ahead. No further involvement unless something changes.
English teachers Use the platform self-service — no training day. Optional 3-min tour video. Escalate any issue to the project owner.
The English Hub Founder-level support within 24h, bi-weekly platform updates, the Week-1 DPO call, and the 48h exit pack on request.

How it's done

  • Web app — students sign in with school email; no install, no IT project
  • Roster uploaded by CSV (or MIS export: iSAMS, Engage, Bromcom, Arbor)
  • Classes set up by exam board, teacher and year group in ~10 min
  • Success criteria agreed in writing before launch — typically marking turnaround, adoption, and teacher NPS
  • One-page agreement; standard DPA fitting UK GDPR + PDPPL; no lock-in
