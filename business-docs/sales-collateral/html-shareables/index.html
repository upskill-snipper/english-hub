Practical notes

How to share these

Email and WhatsApp

Open any document in your browser, press Ctrl/Cmd + P, and save as PDF. The print stylesheet is tuned to A4 with clean margins and page-break-respecting layout. The resulting PDFs are typically 100–250 KB each — well under WhatsApp's fast-share threshold.

Procurement portals

Upload the printed PDFs into the school's procurement system. The compliance section of document 01 plus the full PDMS index (available on request to dpo@theenglishhub.app) is what typically gets requested.

In a conversation

The five documents are sequenced for a conversation: document 01 first, then 02 when the school signals interest, 03 to remove the implementation objection, 04 for the visual tour, 05 for the finance review. Send them in that order rather than all at once.
