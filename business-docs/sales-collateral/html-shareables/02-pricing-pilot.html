What's NOT in a pilot

No auto-renew. No "minimum 3-year" lock-in. No clawback for cancellation. Termination at will by either side with 7 days' written notice during the pilot; refund pro-rated to weeks actually delivered.

Success criteria — agreed in writing in Week 1

How we measure whether the pilot works

We do not run open-ended evaluations. The criteria below are our defaults; we adapt them in the kickoff call to fit what matters most to your department. If we miss the agreed criteria, the school does not renew and owes nothing beyond the pilot fee.

Metric Default target How measured
Department adoption ≥ 70% of teachers active by Week 4 Platform login data
Marking turnaround for the practice cohort From ≥ 7 days → under 1 hour median Platform analytics
Student time on platform per week ≥ 25 minutes per active student Platform analytics
Parent engagement with weekly emails ≥ 40% open rate Email analytics
Teacher NPS at close-out ≥ +30 Anonymous survey
What each side commits to

Pilot responsibilities

What your school commits to

  • One project owner (Head of English or Deputy Head Academic), ~60 minutes per week
  • Three 30-minute calls: kickoff (Week 1), mid-review (Week 4–6), close-out (final week)
  • A student roster — CSV upload or MIS export
  • Honest feedback throughout the pilot

What we commit to

  • Founder-level support within 24 working hours, direct to the project owner
  • Bi-weekly platform updates, prioritising pilot-school requests
  • A Week-1 DPO call with your data-protection lead, separate from the academic kickoff
  • A 48-hour exit pack on request: full data export, deletion confirmation, audit certificate
Procurement-ready compliance

The data-protection position

  • PDPPL Article 17 cross-border consent live in the signup flow as of 20 May 2026
  • Three v1.0 DPIAs on file: processing of children's data, AI features, analytics
  • Article 16 permit application dossier drafted and shelf-ready for NCGAA submission
  • Separately-appointed DPO + DSL
  • EU-region primary database + UK-region backend + zero AI-training on submitted essays (contractual)
  • ICO Children's Code high-privacy defaults enforced in code; strict test coverage
  • Pilot agreement is one page; standard DPA available to your DPO before signature

How to commit

Three steps.

  1. Email dpo@theenglishhub.app with subject "Pilot interest — [your school name]". Tell us scope (year-group / department / multi-campus), year groups, the project-owner name and email, and anyone to cc.
  2. Within 24 hours, we send the one-page pilot agreement, the DPA, and a compliance dossier for your DPO (PDMS index + RoPA + three v1.0 DPIAs + sub-processor list).
  3. Kickoff within 5 working days of countersignature.
