**Controller:** The English Hub Ltd (United Kingdom)

**Date of record:** 2026-05-12
**Review cadence:** Quarterly, or upon material change to subprocessor list or product scope
**Maintained by:** Data Protection Officer, cj@upskillenergy.com

This record is maintained under UK GDPR Article 30 and is produced in a form intended to satisfy Qatar PDPPL Article 5 (records of processing operations) and equivalent obligations in other jurisdictions where the service is offered. Where a processing activity involves data of Qatar residents, the Qatar-specific lawful basis is noted alongside the UK GDPR basis.

**Standing security and governance controls across all activities:**

TLS 1.3 in transit; AES-256 at rest in primary stores; least-privilege access with SSO+MFA for all staff; quarterly access review; subprocessor list reviewed quarterly and on each material change; DPIA on any new activity involving children, AI, or new cross-border flow; breach notification target 72 hours to lead supervisory authority and to NCSA where Qatar residents are affected; vendor DPAs on file for every recipient named above.
