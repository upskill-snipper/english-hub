## 7. How to use this file

- **Never quote a number not in this file.** If you need one, email the founder and ask — and then add it here.
- **Never quote a stale number.** If "last updated" is more than 30 days old, stop. Update first.
- **When the founder is being interviewed,** pre-print the top 10 figures and hand them to the founder on paper. They do not have to memorise them, but they must never guess.
- **When a journalist challenges a number,** send them the source row from this document. Journalists appreciate traceable numbers.
