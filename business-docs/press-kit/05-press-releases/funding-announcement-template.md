# Press Release Template — Funding Announcement

**How to use this template**

- A funding announcement is a news peg, not a product pitch. Lead with the amount, the lead investor, and what the money is for — in that order.
- Never disclose a valuation unless your lead investor has signed off in writing.
- Always coordinate the embargo with your lead investor's comms team.
- Keep the release under 500 words.
- Save as `funding-[YYYY-MM-DD]-[round-type].md` in the `sent/` subfolder when it ships.
