# Press Release Template — Customer Milestone

**How to use this template**

- A milestone release is a small story, told well. It is the kind of news that gets covered by local press, education trade titles, and investor newsletters — not the front page.
- Good milestones: "10,000 students", "100 schools", "1 million essays marked", "first cohort of GCSE results", "a year since launch."
- Bad milestones: "reached an important milestone", "record-breaking quarter" (meaningless without the number).
- Every milestone release must answer one question: *so what?* Why should anyone care?
- Keep it under 400 words.
- Save as `milestone-[YYYY-MM-DD]-[short-slug].md` in the `sent/` folder.
