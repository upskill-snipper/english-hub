**Last updated:** April 2026
**Owner:** Founder / Marketing lead
**Status:** Living document — update every quarter, or after any material change (funding, team, milestones, new product features)

---

## What this kit is for

This press kit is the single source of truth for everything a journalist, partner, investor, PR agency, or podcast host needs to tell The English Hub's story accurately and quickly.

It is designed so that:

- A **journalist** can grab a fact sheet, a founder bio, a headshot, and a boilerplate in under 90 seconds and file a story.
- A **PR agency** can pitch our launch, funding, or milestone news without needing to interview the founder for basics.
- An **investor** can read the one-pager and investor FAQ and decide whether to take a first call.
- The **founder** can copy-paste an elevator pitch into an email without re-writing it every time.

Everything in this kit should be drafted so it is *usable as-is*. Placeholders are clearly marked `[LIKE THIS]` and should be filled in before the kit is sent out.

## How to use this kit

### If you are a journalist or podcast host
Start with:
1. `04-boilerplate/company-boilerplate.md` — the paragraph that ends every press release
2. `01-fact-sheet/fact-sheet.md` — the numbers and product facts
3. `03-founder-story/founder-bio.md` — pick the word count you need
4. `06-media/` — request current screenshots and a headshot

If you need a quote, a demo account, or clarification, contact **press@theenglishhub.app**.

### If you are a PR agency pitching on our behalf
1. Read `02-brand/voice-and-tone.md` before writing anything.
2. Use the relevant template in `05-press-releases/` as a starting point — do not re-invent the structure.
3. Pull supporting stats only from `04-boilerplate/key-statistics.md`. Do not quote any number that is not in that file without founder approval.
4. Log all outreach in `09-tracking/press-outreach-log.csv`.
5. Log all mentions in `09-tracking/media-mentions-log.csv`.

### If you are an investor
1. `08-investor/investor-one-pager.md` — the 60-second read.
2. `08-investor/investor-faq.md` — the deeper dive.
3. If you want a deck or a call, email **founder@theenglishhub.app** with the subject line `[INVESTOR] — [Fund name]`.

### If you are the founder
- Every elevator pitch in `01-fact-sheet/elevator-pitches.md` is memorised *word-for-word* before any press event.
- Every number in `04-boilerplate/key-statistics.md` is something you can say out loud under pressure. If you cannot, fix it.
- Review this kit on the first Monday of every quarter.
