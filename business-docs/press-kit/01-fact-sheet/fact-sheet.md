## Who we are

The English Hub is an AI-powered GCSE and IGCSE English revision platform that gives every student a personal English tutor at 1/50th the cost of traditional tutoring.

## What we do

We combine exam-board-specific courses (AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC, Cambridge IGCSE) with instant AI essay feedback, interactive literature analysis, mock exams, and gamified revision. Students get marked essays in seconds instead of waiting a week for a teacher, and parents get a clear view of progress for less than the price of a single private tutor lesson per month.

## Key features (5 bullets)

1. **AI essay feedback in under 30 seconds**, marked against the official AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC and Cambridge IGCSE mark schemes.
2. **Exam-board-specific courses** for every set text (An Inspector Calls, Macbeth, A Christmas Carol, Power and Conflict, Love and Relationships, Unseen Poetry, and the full Language Paper 1 and Paper 2 skill set).
3. **Interactive revision games** — Grade Climber, Quote Detective, Theme Matcher, Speed Analysis, Spelling Bee, Vocabulary Builder and Comprehension Challenge — that turn revision into 10-minute sessions students actually finish.
4. **Mock exams with auto-marking**, timer, and a personalised "what to revise next" report.
5. **Teacher and parent dashboards** showing time on task, mark-scheme grades, strongest and weakest assessment objectives, and exam-readiness by set text.
