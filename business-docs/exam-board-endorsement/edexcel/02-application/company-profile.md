## 2. What we do

The English Hub is a digital teaching and learning platform for secondary English in UK and international schools. It supports English teachers, students and independent learners working towards GCSE and International GCSE English Language and English Literature qualifications.

The platform combines structured lesson sequences, teacher-ready worked examples, annotated text extracts, formative tasks, and revision tools. Content is produced by a core editorial team of qualified English teachers and examiners, working to an internal editorial standard.

## 3. Why we exist

Our founding observation is that secondary English teachers spend a disproportionate amount of their preparation time recreating or re-scaffolding the same core material every year — analysis of set texts, worked responses to past questions, model paragraphs, teaching sequences for specific assessment objectives. The English Hub exists to give teachers a trusted, specification-accurate library of that material so they can spend more of their time on the aspects of teaching that only a teacher can do.

## 10. Why we want Pearson Edexcel endorsement

Endorsement matters to us for three reasons:

1. **Quality signal for teachers.** Heads of English are cautious about adopting a new resource without independent validation. Endorsement gives them that validation from the awarding body itself.
2. **Fairness to students.** We want the material our users rely on to be traceable back to an independent subject review. Endorsement makes that traceability explicit.
3. **Sustainability.** Endorsement unlocks the commercial routes that let us invest more in editorial quality — which is good for teachers, good for students, and good for Pearson's brand as an exam board.

We would be proud to be an endorsed resource for Pearson Edexcel and we are ready to meet the quality bar.
