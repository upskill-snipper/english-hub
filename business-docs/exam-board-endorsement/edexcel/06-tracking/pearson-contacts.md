## 5. Contact route of choice

First contact is by email to `publisher.relations@pearson.com`, addressed to "Publisher Relations Team" as we have no confirmed named lead yet. A follow-up is scheduled for 10 working days if there is no acknowledgement.

We do **not** do the following as first-contact routes:

- LinkedIn DM to a named individual (too informal for a publisher application)
- Twitter / X
- Cold call to Pearson switchboard
- Any contact with examiners whose role is live assessment (conflict of interest risk)

## 6. Escalation routes

If no acknowledgement is received after 15 working days of the first email, escalate in this order:

1. Polite reminder to `publisher.relations@pearson.com` referencing the original email.
2. Email to a confirmed head of function (once identified) with the subject line "Follow-up on publisher endorsement enquiry".
3. Letter by post to the Pearson Qualifications registered office.
4. Final: approach through a mutually-known intermediary in UK educational publishing.
