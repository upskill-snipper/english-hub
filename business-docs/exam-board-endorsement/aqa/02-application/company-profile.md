## 2. Mission

The English Hub exists to make high-quality, specification-accurate GCSE English teaching and revision available to every student, regardless of the school they attend or the budget their family can afford.

Our core belief: GCSE English should be taught with both rigour and humanity. That means mapping tightly to the AQA specification so students meet the assessment criteria, while treating the literature on the specification as real writing worth reading — not as a set of command-word exercises.

## 3. What we do

The English Hub is a web-based learning platform covering the AQA GCSE English Literature (8702) and GCSE English Language (8700) specifications. The platform offers:

- Set-text study units (Shakespeare, 19th-century novel, modern texts, poetry anthology options as set out in the 8702 specification)
- Language Paper 1 and Paper 2 teaching sequences, including unseen-extract analysis, creative writing, and transactional writing
- Spoken Language Endorsement guidance and student resources
- Assessment practice with model answers at multiple grade levels
- Teacher-facing resources including lesson plans, schemes of work, and quiz mark schemes
- A parent and student dashboard showing progress against specification coverage

## 9. Why we want to work with AQA

We have deliberately built The English Hub around the AQA specifications because AQA is the dominant board for GCSE English Literature and Language in the schools we serve. Our users — students, teachers, and parents — trust the AQA brand. Formal AQA approval would:

1. Give those users verified assurance that the resource they are using has been checked by AQA's subject experts.
2. Give AQA a credible, modern, digital-native resource that meets students where they actually learn.
3. Support AQA's own mission to drive up standards in GCSE English by making high-quality aligned resources widely available.

We are committed to the approval process on AQA's terms, at AQA's pace, and with full acceptance of AQA's editorial and trademark requirements.
