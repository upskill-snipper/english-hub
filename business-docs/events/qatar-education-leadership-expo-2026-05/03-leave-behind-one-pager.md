## What we do

We mark GCSE and IGCSE English Language and Literature practice essays with AI, against the **actual mark schemes** of all six exam boards your students sit:

**AQA · Pearson Edexcel · OCR · Eduqas · WJEC · Cambridge IGCSE**

A student submits a 600-word essay. Within about a minute they get:

- A suggested band against the assessment objectives the mark scheme uses
- Paragraph-level feedback referencing those AOs by name
- Concrete suggestions of what to fix and what to keep

Teachers stay the final authority on any grade that goes on a report. What the AI returns is **formative practice feedback** — not an awarded grade, not a predicted grade, not a substitute for examiner judgement.

## Why it matters for your English department

A department of 80–120 KS4 students writing fortnightly practice essays is generating 160–240 essays a month. Real-world marking turnaround in most departments is **7–14 days**. Our customers cut that to under a minute for the practice cohort, and reclaim teacher marking time for the essays that genuinely need a human eye — coursework, controlled assessment, summative tasks.
