## Sending discipline — the rules

- **Send within 48 hours of the event end.** Friday at the latest. Anything later than that and the founder face fades in their memory.
- **Personalise every Tier 1/2 email by hand.** The line that personalises is "appreciated you taking the time to chat about [their words]" — that line is what the recipient looks at first.
- **One email, two attachments, one ask.** Don't bury the ask in a five-paragraph email.
- **Use the DPO email.** It signals seriousness about data protection and it's already the address on the leave-behind.
- **Track replies in the lead-capture spreadsheet.** Every reply moves the contact to "engaged" status; every non-reply after 7 days gets a single bump email; every non-reply after the bump is moved to "cold" — don't keep emailing.
