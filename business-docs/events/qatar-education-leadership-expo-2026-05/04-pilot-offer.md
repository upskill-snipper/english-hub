## The offer in one paragraph

For the first 10 schools in our Founding Schools cohort, we run a **one-term pilot at £3,000–£4,000**, with **defined success criteria agreed in writing before kickoff**, **no auto-renew**, and a clear path to a £4,000/year annual deployment if the pilot meets its criteria. Schools that complete the pilot stay on the Founding price (£4,000/year) **for the life of their licence**, even after our standard price rises to £8,000 in August 2026.

## The three pilot scopes

| Scope | Indicative price | Who it's for | What it covers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A — Year-group pilot** | from **£2,500** | A single year group (e.g. one Y10 or one Y11 IGCSE cohort) — 60–120 students typically | Full platform access for that cohort; teacher dashboard limited to that year's classes |
| **B — Department pilot** | from **£4,000** | The whole English department, Y7–Y13 — typically 300–800 students | Full platform access for every student studying English at the school; whole-department teacher dashboard |
| **C — Multi-campus / group pilot** | **custom** (no fixed floor) | Education group running 2+ campuses (e.g. Doha British School chain, ISP, Nord Anglia, GEMS regional) | Negotiated per group — usually a 2-campus proof-of-concept escalating to group rollout |

*Indicative founder pricing. Final pricing depends on school size, scope, and rollout requirements.*

**Pilot length:** approximately one term (8–12 weeks). Most pilots are scoped to fit a natural half-term boundary so the close-out review lands before holidays.

## Success criteria — agreed in writing before we kick off

We do not run "open-ended evaluations." Before any pilot starts, we agree, in writing, the specific metrics by which the pilot will be judged. Typical criteria for an English department pilot are:

| Metric | Typical target | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| % of department adopting the platform | ≥ 70% of teachers active in week 4 | Tells you the tool isn't sitting on the shelf |
| Average essay marking turnaround for the practice cohort | from ≥ 7 days → under 1 hour median | The headline operational outcome |
| Student time-on-platform per week | ≥ 25 minutes per active student | Tells you students are actually using it, not just signing up |
| Parent engagement with weekly emails | ≥ 40% open rate | Tells you the parent channel is working |
| Teacher Net Promoter Score at close-out | ≥ +30 | Tells you the department wants to renew |

These are **defaults**. We adapt them to your school's specific situation in the kickoff call. **If we miss the agreed criteria, you do not renew, and you owe nothing beyond the pilot fee.** No auto-renew. No "minimum 3-year lock-in." No clawback for cancellation.

## What we ask of the school during the pilot

- Name **one school-side project owner** (typically the Head of English or Deputy Head Academic) who will commit roughly 60 minutes a week of attention to the pilot
- A **30-minute kickoff call** in week 1 — class setup, agree success criteria, walkthrough
- A **30-minute mid-pilot review** in week 4–6
- A **30-minute close-out review** in the final week — go/no-go on annual deployment
- Honest feedback throughout — what's working, what isn't, what to change

That's it. No teacher-training sessions, no "implementation consultant" charge, no integration project — the platform is designed to be self-service for a teacher who can use a web browser.

## After the pilot — annual deployment

If the pilot meets the agreed success criteria and you choose to continue, you convert to annual deployment at the Founding Schools rate **for the life of your licence**:

- **£4,000/year** per school for whole-school access (Y7–Y13 English)
- Standard price from August 2026 will be **£8,000/year**; you are grandfathered at £4k

If your school has more than 1,000 students or runs multiple campuses, we move to custom pricing — typically **from £6,000–£12,000/year** for mid-size schools and **from £25,000/year** for large schools or education groups. The £4k rate is for single-campus schools up to ~1,000 students.

## What's NOT in the pilot fee

- Sworn translation of any document into Modern Standard Arabic (we provide an informational MSA translation; a sworn translation can be commissioned separately, ~£500–£1,500)
- Bespoke integrations with school-side MIS (iSAMS, Engage, etc.) — these are scoped separately
- On-site training visits — the platform is designed to be self-service; if you want a half-day on-site, we can do it for travel + £750

## The legal framework around the pilot

- **One-page pilot agreement** — not a 40-page MSA. Drafted, signed within 48 hours of you saying yes.
- **Data Processing Agreement** (DPA) — our standard, fits UK GDPR + PDPPL. Available to your DPO on request before signature.
- **Termination at will** by either side with 7 days' notice during the pilot, refund pro-rated to weeks actually delivered.
- **Governing law:** England and Wales for the pilot. We can move to Qatar law for the annual deployment if your procurement requires it.
