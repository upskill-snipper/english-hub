# Critical Flags -- All Verified Errors
# Complete list of the most common mistakes found on revision sites

1. OZYMANDIAS: 'Look ON my Works' NOT 'Look UPON my works'
2. LA BELLE DAME: TWO versions exist -- Edexcel uses Indicator 1820 version NOT Milnes 1848
3. EXPOSURE (Owen): 'knive' in Line 1 IS CORRECT -- not a typo or error
4. CHARGE OF THE LIGHT BRIGADE: 'Theirs not to REASON why' NOT 'question why'
5. FRANKENSTEIN: 'It's alive!' is FILM ONLY -- never appears in the novel
6. ANIMAL FARM: Treat as restricted -- Orwell estate actively enforces despite UK PD status
7. DRUMMER HODGE: Set during SECOND BOER WAR (South Africa, 1899) -- NOT World War One
8. EDEN ROCK: The place is INVENTED by Causley -- he confirmed 'I have no idea, I mean I made it up!'
9. BLESSING (Dharker): Set in India -- NOT Africa
10. WALKING AWAY: Dedicated to SEAN Day-Lewis (eldest son) -- NOT Daniel Day-Lewis the actor
11. C. DAY-LEWIS: Surname is HYPHENATED -- 'Day-Lewis' not 'Day Lewis'
12. EDEXCEL IGCSE ANTHOLOGY ISSUE 2: 'Half-Caste' uses 'yu' not 'you' -- 'Bright Lights of Sarajevo' has extra stanza breaks
13. NON-FICTION ADAPTED TEXTS: 'Explorers or boys messing about?' and 'Young and dyslexic?' differ from their online originals -- use anthology version only
14. ZEPHANIAH: Died January 2023 -- rights now held by estate
15. ALAGIAH: Died July 2023 -- rights now held by estate
16. THE PRELUDE (AQA): AQA uses 1850 Prelude; OCR uses 1799 Prelude -- DIFFERENT TEXTS
17. FUNERAL BLUES (Auden): Use 1940 REVISED version NOT 1936 'Twelve Songs IX' version
18. CAMBRIDGE IGCSE 2026-27: Set texts not yet confirmed -- flag as pending
19. BAYONET CHARGE: Hughes born 1930 -- did NOT fight in WWI. Poem imagined from father's accounts.
20. CHECKING OUT ME HISTORY: In P&C anthology -- NOT Love & Relationships (common misclassification)
21. THE EMIGREE: Title requires accents on BOTH final e's -- 'Emigree' is wrong; correct is 'Emigree' with accents
22. THE EMIGREE: City is UNNAMED intentionally -- do NOT identify as Sarajevo or any specific city
23. REMAINS (Armitage): Armitage never served in any war -- poem is imagined, based on research
24. BAYONET CHARGE: 'etcetera' (single) is canonical per Faber 1957 'The Hawk in the Rain' -- cite EXACTLY as printed; do NOT abbreviate to 'etc.' and do NOT double to 'etcetera etcetera' (the doubled form is a propagated PMT Education error).
25. TISSUE (Dharker): Final line 'turned into your skin.' stands ALONE as isolated single-line stanza
26. HOLY THURSDAY (Blake / OCR): TWO poems with this title -- verify which version (Innocence=joyful; Experience=satirical)
27. BOAT STEALING (OCR): OCR uses 1799 TWO-PART PRELUDE -- NOT the standard 1850 edition
28. DECOMPOSITION (Ghose): Low documentation -- verify all content against Eduqas anthology edition
29. ROBERT FROST: UK copyright expires 2033/34 -- treat as RESTRICTED for UK platform
30. THEME FOR ENGLISH B (Hughes): UK copyright expires 2038 -- RESTRICTED; do not assume public domain
