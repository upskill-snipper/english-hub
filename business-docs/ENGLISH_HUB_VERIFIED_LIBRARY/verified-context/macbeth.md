# Macbeth -- Verified Context
Author: William Shakespeare (1564-1616). Written c.1606. PUBLIC DOMAIN.
Sources: Holinshed's Chronicles (1587). First printed 1623 First Folio.

Historical context: Written for James I, who was fascinated by witchcraft and was himself Scottish. The Gunpowder Plot (1605) context underpins themes of treachery and regicide.

Literary context: Tragedy. 5 acts. Soliloquies reveal psychological states. Ambiguity throughout -- is Macbeth a victim of fate or of free will?

Key passages for AQA: Act 1 Scene 7 (ambition soliloquy), Act 2 Scene 2 (murder aftermath), Act 3 Scene 1 (Banquo), Act 5 Scene 5 (nihilism).

Themes: Ambition and corruption; guilt; appearance vs reality; gender and power; fate vs free will.

Exam: AQA GCSE Set Text Paper 1.
