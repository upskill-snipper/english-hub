# Tissue -- Verified Context
Author: Imtiaz Dharker (b.1954, Lahore, Pakistan; raised Glasgow). Published: The Terrorist at My Table (Bloodaxe, 2006).

Historical context: Dharker's multicultural background. Questions human constructs of power: maps, buildings, financial records, borders.

Literary context: Mostly quatrains + ONE isolated final line: 'turned into your skin.' Extended metaphor: paper = human life/skin. Fragility of all seemingly powerful things.

CRITICAL: Final line stands alone as a single-line stanza -- do not attach it to previous stanza.

Themes: Power and fragility; human constructs; identity; impermanence.

Exam: AQA P&C. Compare: Ozymandias (powerful things destroyed by time).
