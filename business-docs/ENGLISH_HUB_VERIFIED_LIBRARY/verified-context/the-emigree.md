# The Emigree -- Verified Context
Author: Carol Rumens (b.1944, South London). Visiting Professor, Bangor University.
Published: Thinking of Skins (Bloodaxe, 1993).
CRITICAL: City is DELIBERATELY UNNAMED -- do NOT identify as Sarajevo.

Historical context: Written 1993 during Yugoslav wars. Context intentionally universal.

Literary context: 3 stanzas of 8 lines. Free verse. Central metaphor: memory = sunlight -- warm, persistent, cannot be suppressed by tyranny or censorship.

CRITICAL on title: Accents required on BOTH final e's -- correct spelling is Emigree with grave accents.

Themes: Displacement; memory and identity; censorship; linguistic identity; exile.

Exam: AQA P&C. Compare: Checking Out Me History (identity), Kamikaze (cultural identity).
