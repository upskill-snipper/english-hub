# A Christmas Carol -- Verified Context
Author: Charles Dickens (1812-1870). Published 19 December 1843. PUBLIC DOMAIN.

Historical context: Direct response to the 1843 Children's Employment Commission Report on child labour. Dickens had worked in a blacking factory at age 12. Poverty and the Poor Laws (1834) are central targets.

Literary context: 5 staves (chapters). Gothic, supernatural, sentimental registers. Scrooge = Victorian capitalist; Cratchits = deserving poor; Tiny Tim = emotional focus. Three ghosts = past/present/future = memory/social reality/consequences.

Themes: Social responsibility; redemption; poverty; greed; the Christmas spirit.

Exam: AQA GCSE Set Text Paper 1. Compare: Jekyll & Hyde (duality), An Inspector Calls (responsibility).
