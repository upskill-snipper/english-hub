# Bayonet Charge -- Verified Context
Author: Ted Hughes (1930-1998). Published: The Hawk in the Rain (Faber, 1957).
CRITICAL: Hughes born 1930 -- never in WWI. Poem imagined from father Bill Hughes's accounts.

Historical context: Bill Hughes survived the Battle of Ypres -- one of only two survivors from his regiment.

Literary context: 3 stanzas. Begins mid-action with no context. The hare (stanza 2) is shot -- a substitute/proxy for the soldier. Single 'etcetera' (Faber 1957 canonical; do NOT double, do NOT abbreviate to 'etc.') -- devastating dismissal of all abstract reasons for war.

Themes: War; loss of innocence; survival instinct; disillusionment; futility.

Exam: AQA P&C. Compare: Exposure, Charge of the Light Brigade.
