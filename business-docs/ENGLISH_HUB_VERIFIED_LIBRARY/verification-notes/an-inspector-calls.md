# An Inspector Calls -- Verification Notes
Common wrong quote: 'We do not live alone' -- correct is 'We do not live alone. We are members of one body.'
Full Inspector quote must include the second sentence for full credit in analysis.

Setting error: Play is set in April 1912 -- the SAME month and year the Titanic sank. This is not coincidental.

Character error: Calling Sheila 'unchanged' at the end. Sheila is one of the characters who DOES change -- she accepts responsibility. Eric also changes.

Common misattribution: 'We are all responsible for each other' is a paraphrase. The EXACT quote is 'We are members of one body. We are responsible for each other.'
