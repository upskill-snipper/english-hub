# Ozymandias -- Verification Notes
Most common online error: 'Look UPON my works' -- WRONG. Text reads 'Look ON my Works'
Source: Project Gutenberg, Wikisource, Oxford World's Classics -- all confirmed 'Look ON'

Punctuation variant: Some editions use quotation marks differently around lines 10-14. AQA and Edexcel use slightly different punctuation in their anthology versions. Always verify against the specific board anthology, not external sources.

Common wrong technique: Students call the sneer 'pathetic fallacy' -- it is personification, not pathetic fallacy.
