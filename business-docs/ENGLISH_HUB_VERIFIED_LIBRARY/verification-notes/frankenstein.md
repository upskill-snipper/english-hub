# Frankenstein -- Verification Notes
'It's alive!' -- FILM ONLY (James Whale's 1931 film). This phrase does NOT appear anywhere in Mary Shelley's novel. DO NOT USE.

The creature is NEVER named Frankenstein. Frankenstein is the scientist. The creature has no name.

Common error: Saying Frankenstein is a Gothic novel only. It is also a Romantic novel and a proto-science fiction novel.

Published 1818 (anonymously), revised 1831. The 1831 edition has significant changes from 1818. Most school editions use 1818 text.
