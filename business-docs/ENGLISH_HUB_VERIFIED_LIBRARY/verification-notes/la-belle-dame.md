# La Belle Dame sans Merci -- Verification Notes
TWO VERSIONS EXIST:
- Indicator 1820 (published during Keats's lifetime): 'O what can ail thee, knight-at-arms'
- Milnes 1848 (published after Keats's death): 'Ah, what can ail thee, wretched wight'

Edexcel IGCSE Anthology uses the INDICATOR 1820 VERSION.
Do not use the Milnes version for Edexcel platform content.

Line count: 48 lines (12 stanzas of 4 lines each).

Common error: Identifying the lady as a specific historical or mythological figure. She is deliberately ambiguous.
