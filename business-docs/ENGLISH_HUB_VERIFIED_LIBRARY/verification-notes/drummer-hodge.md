# Drummer Hodge -- Verification Notes
CRITICAL: This poem is about the Second Boer War (South Africa, 1899) NOT World War One.
Students and revision sites frequently misidentify the war.

Glossary terms are important for analysis:
- kopje = small rocky hill (Afrikaans)
- veldt = open grassland (South Africa)
- Karoo = vast semi-desert plateau
- gloam = twilight, dusk

Hardy never visited South Africa -- the poem is imagined.

Do not confuse with 'Drummer Boy' poems generally -- this is a specific historical figure (no specific soldier is recorded, but 'Drummer Hodge' = generic Wessex farm boy).
