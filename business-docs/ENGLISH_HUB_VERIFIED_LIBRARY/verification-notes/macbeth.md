# Macbeth -- Verification Notes
Act/Scene references: Always cite Act, Scene, and approximate line. 'Act 1 Scene 7' is Lady Macbeth's 'unsex me here' speech. Do not confuse with Act 1 Scene 5 (letter soliloquy).

Common wrong quote: 'Out, out damned spot!' -- the CORRECT quote is 'Out, damned spot! Out, I say!' (Act 5 Scene 1, Lady Macbeth's sleepwalking).

Common wrong quote: 'Double double toil and trouble' -- correct is 'Double, double toil and trouble' (commas after each 'double').

Macbeth's ambition: He has it BEFORE the witches -- Act 1 Scene 4 ('Stars, hide your fires') is before he meets Lady Macbeth in Act 1 Scene 5. Students often say Lady Macbeth or the witches CREATE his ambition -- they amplify it.
