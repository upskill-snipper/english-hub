# Charge of the Light Brigade -- Verification Notes
Line 14: 'Theirs not to REASON why' -- confirmed Project Gutenberg.
Common online error: 'Theirs not to QUESTION why' -- this is WRONG.

Context error: Some sites say Tennyson glorifies the charge. He does not -- the repeated 'blunder'd' and the tone are ambiguous. This is an exam debate.

Do not confuse with 'Dulce et Decorum Est' (Owen) -- they are different poems about different wars (Crimea vs WWI).
