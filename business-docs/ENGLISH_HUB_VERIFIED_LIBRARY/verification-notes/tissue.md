# Tissue -- Verification Notes
Final line: 'turned into your skin.' is a SINGLE LINE STANZA standing alone. Do not attach it to the previous stanza.

Common error: Saying the poem is only about maps or buildings. The final line reveals the whole poem is about human bodies and human life.

Common error: Calling Dharker Indian. She was born in Lahore (now Pakistan), raised in Glasgow. Dual British-Pakistani identity.
