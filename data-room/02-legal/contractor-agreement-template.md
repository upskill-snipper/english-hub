## 1. Services

1.1 The Contractor shall provide the services described in Schedule 1 (the "**Services**") to the Company with reasonable skill and care and in accordance with good industry practice.

1.2 The Contractor shall determine the manner in which the Services are performed, subject to the Company's reasonable directions regarding deliverables, deadlines, quality, and safety.

## 2. Status and IR35

2.1 The Parties intend and agree that the Contractor is engaged as an independent contractor and that nothing in this Agreement creates a relationship of employer/employee, worker, agent, partnership or joint venture between the Parties.

2.2 The Contractor confirms that:

(a) it is in business on its own account;
(b) it bears its own financial risk in the provision of the Services;
(c) it provides its own equipment where practical;
(d) it is free to provide services to other clients, provided this does not conflict with this Agreement;
(e) it has the right of substitution set out in clause 2.3.

2.3 The Contractor may, with the Company's prior written consent (such consent not to be unreasonably withheld), provide a suitably qualified substitute to perform the Services, provided the Contractor remains responsible for the substitute's acts and omissions and continues to pay the substitute.

2.4 The Contractor is responsible for all income tax, National Insurance contributions, and other taxes in respect of the fees paid under this Agreement, and shall indemnify the Company against any liability, assessment or claim made against the Company in respect of such taxes, including any charge under the off-payroll working rules (commonly known as "IR35") in Chapter 10, Part 2 of ITEPA 2003.

2.5 If the Company is required to determine the Contractor's status under the off-payroll working rules, the Contractor shall cooperate in good faith and provide any information reasonably requested.

## 3. Term and termination

3.1 This Agreement starts on REPLACE WITH START DATE and continues until REPLACE WITH END DATE or until terminated in accordance with this clause.

3.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving REPLACE WITH NOTICE PERIOD (e.g. 30 days') written notice.

3.3 The Company may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect by written notice if the Contractor:

(a) commits a material breach of this Agreement that is not remedied within 10 business days of notice;
(b) acts in a way that, in the Company's reasonable opinion, brings the Company or its brand into disrepute;
(c) becomes insolvent, enters into an arrangement with creditors, or is unable to pay its debts as they fall due;
(d) commits any act of dishonesty or material negligence.

3.4 On termination, the Contractor shall (i) return all Company property and confidential information; (ii) deliver all work in progress; and (iii) be paid for Services properly performed up to the date of termination.

## 4. Fees

4.1 The Company shall pay the Contractor the fees set out in Schedule 1, against a valid VAT invoice where applicable, within REPLACE WITH PAYMENT TERMS (e.g. 30 days) of receipt.

4.2 The Contractor is responsible for its own expenses unless agreed in writing in advance.

## 5. Intellectual property

5.1 All intellectual property rights in any materials, code, content, designs, curriculum, brand assets, or other works created or developed by the Contractor in the course of providing the Services ("**Deliverables**") shall vest in the Company on creation.

5.2 To the extent that any such rights do not vest automatically, the Contractor hereby assigns them, with full title guarantee and by way of present assignment of future rights, to the Company absolutely.

5.3 The Contractor waives all moral rights in the Deliverables in favour of the Company to the extent permitted by law.

5.4 The Contractor shall execute any further documents and take any further actions the Company reasonably requires to perfect its title to the Deliverables.

5.5 The Contractor warrants that the Deliverables shall not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party and shall indemnify the Company against any claim arising from a breach of this warranty.

## 6. Confidentiality

6.1 The Contractor shall keep confidential all information of a commercially sensitive nature disclosed to it by the Company ("**Confidential Information**") and shall use it only to perform the Services.

6.2 This obligation continues for REPLACE WITH DURATION (e.g. 3 years) after termination of this Agreement.

6.3 Confidential Information does not include information that is or becomes public other than by breach of this clause, or that the Contractor is required to disclose by law.

## 7. Data protection

7.1 The Parties shall comply with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Where the Contractor processes personal data on the Company's behalf, the Parties shall enter into a data processing agreement meeting the requirements of Article 28 UK GDPR.

## 8. Liability and insurance

8.1 The Contractor shall maintain adequate professional indemnity and public liability insurance during the term of this Agreement.

8.2 Neither Party excludes or limits liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud, or any other liability that cannot lawfully be limited.

## 9. General

9.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements on its subject matter.

9.2 No variation of this Agreement is effective unless in writing and signed by both Parties.

9.3 A person who is not a party to this Agreement has no rights under the Contracts (Rights of Third Parties) Act 1999.

9.4 This Agreement is governed by the law of England and Wales, and the Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
