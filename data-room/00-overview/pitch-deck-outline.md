# The English Hub — Pitch Deck Outline (15 slides)

**Status**: Template v0.1
**Format**: 16:9, designed to be read in 3 minutes and presented in 10.
**Rule**: one idea per slide. No dense paragraphs. The speaker adds context; the slide only anchors it.

> **Founder checklist**
> - [ ] All 15 slides drafted with real numbers
> - [ ] Slide 4 (traction) is the strongest slide — test this first
> - [ ] Every claim has a source or is a primary metric from our model
> - [ ] Deck exports cleanly to PDF and is under 10MB
> - [ ] Send-version has an appendix with detail for questions
