## Compliance Framework

The English Hub operates in the UK and EU and processes the personal data of children (ages 13–16). Our documentation is built around:

| Framework | Relevance |
|---|---|
| **UK GDPR** | Primary data protection framework post-Brexit |
| **Data Protection Act 2018 (DPA 2018)** | UK implementation of GDPR; sets age of consent at 13 |
| **Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code)** | 15 mandatory standards for online services likely to be accessed by children |
| **PECR (Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003)** | Cookies, direct marketing |
| **EU GDPR (Regulation 2016/679)** | Applies to EU-based users; digital consent age varies by member state (13–16) |
| **Keeping Children Safe in Education (KCSIE)** | DfE statutory safeguarding guidance |
| **DfE Data Protection Toolkit for Schools** | Sector-specific guidance for ed-tech suppliers |
| **ICO Guidance on AI and data protection** | Relevant to AI-assisted marking and feedback features |
