## Section 4 — Edits, derivatives, and storage

- The English Hub may crop, colour-correct, overlay text on, caption, add motion graphics to, reframe, or otherwise edit the material for publication.
- The English Hub will NOT substantially alter the meaning of your words or likeness, NOR use face-swap / deepfake / AI-voice-clone techniques.
- Material may be stored on UK-resident cloud storage (primary: Supabase UK, AWS London) and may be processed via US-resident sub-processors (Meta, Google, TikTok, YouTube, Vercel) where published for advertising, subject to the Transfer Impact Assessments on file.

## Section 7 — Revocation

You may revoke this release at any time by emailing `privacy@theenglishhub.app`. On receipt:

- Active publication: we will take down within **14 days** from the website and our owned social channels
- Paid advertising: we will cease running ads featuring you within **30 days** (to accommodate ad-platform review cycles)
- Printed collateral: we will not reprint. Existing printed copies will be allowed to cycle out of circulation naturally.
- Archived footage: a copy will be retained solely for audit / defence for 2 years post-revocation, inaccessible to marketing teams.

Withdrawal does not retroactively make prior use unlawful; it stops future use.

## Section 8 — Children (under 18)

If you are under 18, this release is invalid without a parent/guardian's co-signature. The English Hub follows the ICO Children's Code and applies additional safeguards for under-18s:

- **No paid advertising use without parent consent renewed within the last 12 months**
- **Face-blurring available on request at any time**
- **No use in AI training, image-generation datasets, or any model-training pipeline**
- **No use that could identify the child's school location via visible uniform, signage, or geotag**
