## Hi. This page is for you.

We're The English Hub. We built this to help you smash GCSE English. This page tells you — in plain English — what information we collect about you, why, and what you can do about it.

The law says companies have to be clear with young people about data, and we agree. So here it is, short and honest.

---

## Who we are

We're a company in the UK called **The English Hub Ltd**. If you want to contact us, the addresses are at the bottom of this page.

---

## What we know about you

Here's what we have on you and where it comes from:

**Stuff you gave us**
- Your name and email
- Your school and year group
- Your date of birth (so we know you're the right age)
- Your exam board (AQA, Edexcel, OCR, or Eduqas)
- Anything you've written in the app — essays, answers, quiz replies

**Stuff the app makes while you use it**
- Your scores and progress
- Which topics you've finished and which you haven't
- How long you've spent on things
- Feedback the AI wrote on your essays
- Your device type and browser (for making the app work properly)

**Stuff from other people**
- If your school signed you up, they gave us your name, year group, and class
- If you logged in with Google or Microsoft, they told us your name and email

---

## What we do with it

We use your information to:

- **Let you log in and use the app.** Obviously.
- **Show you the right content** for your exam board and year.
- **Give you feedback on your writing.** We use AI for this, and we'll always tell you when it's AI.
- **Track your progress** so you can see how you're improving.
- **Show your teacher your progress** (only if your school signed you up).
- **Keep the app safe** from spam, cheating, and bad actors.
- **Fix bugs and make the app better.**

---

## What we DON'T do

We don't:

- **Sell your data.** Ever. To anyone.
- **Show you ads.** There are no ads in The English Hub.
- **Track you around the internet.** No creepy follow-you-around stuff.
- **Share your essays with other students.**
- **Let the AI learn from your writing.** Our AI provider is contractually blocked from training on your data.
- **Ask you for your phone number, location, or photos** (unless you choose to add a profile picture).
- **Build a behaviour profile of you** for advertising.
- **Do "dark patterns"** — nudging, pressuring, or tricking you into sharing more.

---

## Your writing and the AI

We use AI to help mark your writing and give you feedback. Here's what you should know:

1. **It's not a real exam mark.** Think of it like a helpful study buddy, not a real examiner.
2. **It can make mistakes.** If something looks wrong, tell your teacher.
3. **A human can always override it.** Your teacher has the final say.
4. **It's labelled.** If feedback is from the AI, we tell you.
5. **Your writing is not used to train AI.**

## Who can see your stuff

- **You.** Obviously.
- **Your teachers** (if your school signed you up).
- **Your parents or guardians** if they ask and we can confirm they're your parent/guardian.
- **Our team**, but only the people who actually need to see it to fix bugs or help you — not randomly.
- **The services we use** to run the app (like the company that hosts our servers). They're contracted to keep your data safe and only use it to help us.

We will only give your data to the police or social services if the law requires it, or if we think someone is in danger.

## Your rights — things you can actually do

You have real rights under UK law. Here they are in normal words:

| Your right | What it actually means |
|---|---|
| **See what we have on you** | Ask us for a copy of your data. We have to send it to you within a month. |
| **Fix things that are wrong** | If we've got something wrong, tell us and we'll correct it. |
| **Have your data deleted** | Want us to delete your stuff? Ask. (There are some things we have to keep by law, but we'll tell you.) |
| **Take your data with you** | We'll give you a file you can take to another service. |
| **Say no to certain things** | You can opt out of some processing — ask us and we'll explain. |
| **Change your mind** | If you said yes to something, you can say no later. |
| **Complain** | If you don't like how we've handled your data, you can complain to us or the UK regulator (ICO). |

**You don't need your parents' permission to exercise these rights.** They're your rights, over your data.

To use any of these rights, email **privacy@englishhub.`[tld]`** and tell us what you want.

## Cookies (the internet kind, not biscuits)

Cookies are small files that let websites remember things about you. We use some cookies to make the app work (like keeping you logged in) — we can't turn those off without breaking the app.

We also use a small number of cookies to help us see how people use the app so we can fix bugs and make it better. These are **off by default** for student accounts.

**We do not run advertising cookies on student accounts. Full stop.**

You can change your choices in **Settings → Privacy**.

## How long we keep your stuff

Roughly:
- While your account is active — we keep your stuff so the app works.
- If you stop using the app, we delete or anonymise your work after about a year.
- If you close your account, we delete most stuff within 30 days. Some things (like billing records) we have to keep for longer by law.

The full list is in our [data retention schedule](./data-retention-schedule.md).
