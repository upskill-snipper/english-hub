## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the service. We also use similar technologies (localStorage, pixels, SDKs) and treat them the same way in this policy.

## 2. Our approach to cookies for children

The English Hub is an education service used by children. In line with the **UK Children's Code**:

- We do **not** set marketing or behavioural advertising cookies on accounts identified as belonging to a child.
- We do **not** share data with ad-tech providers.
- Analytics are pseudonymised and aggregated.
- Consent choices are easy to change and available in plain language.

## 3. The cookies we use

### Category 1 — Strictly necessary (no consent required)

These cookies are essential for the service to work. They cannot be switched off.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `eh_session` | The English Hub | Keeps you logged in | Session |
| `eh_csrf` | The English Hub | Protects forms from cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `eh_auth` | The English Hub | Authentication token | 30 days |
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare | Bot management & DDoS protection | 30 minutes |
| `eh_consent` | The English Hub | Stores your cookie preferences | 12 months |

**Legal basis:** Not required under PECR (these are strictly necessary to provide a service you requested).

### Category 2 — Analytics (consent required)

These help us understand how the service is used so we can improve it. For children's accounts these are **off by default**.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `ph_*` | PostHog | Product analytics (pseudonymised; no child profiles; EU hosting) | 12 months |
| `eh_feat_flag` | The English Hub | Feature flag assignment | 30 days |

**Legal basis:** Consent (PECR reg 6 + Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR).

### Category 3 — Marketing (consent required — adults only)

We use marketing cookies **only** on pages targeted at adults (pricing, parents, schools). They are **never set** on student-facing pages.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics (marketing site only) | Campaign measurement on our marketing site | 24 months |
| `li_*` | LinkedIn Insight (marketing site only) | Advertising on LinkedIn targeting schools/teachers | 24 months |

> We do **not** run behavioural advertising to users under 18. Where any doubt exists, marketing cookies are not set.

**Legal basis:** Consent.

## 4. How to manage cookies

- **In-product:** Open **Settings → Privacy → Cookie preferences** to change your choices at any time.
- **On the marketing site:** Click "Cookie settings" in the footer.
- **In your browser:** You can block or delete cookies via your browser's settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will break the service.
- **Do Not Track:** We honour the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal where your browser sends it.

## 5. Third-party tools

Where a third party sets a cookie via our site (e.g. Cloudflare, PostHog), their own privacy policy also applies. We maintain data processing agreements with each provider — see `subprocessor-register.csv`.
