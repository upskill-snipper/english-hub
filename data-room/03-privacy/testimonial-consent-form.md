## Section 5 — How long will we keep your testimonial?

Your testimonial, photograph, and associated data will be retained for as long as we use them in marketing materials, plus a reasonable archival period.

- **Active use:** typically up to 3 years after the date of this consent, unless you withdraw earlier.
- **Archive:** a further 2 years post-active-use, solely as evidence of past consent and for defence against any legal claim.
- **Total default retention: 5 years** from the date of signature.

You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 6).

## Section 6 — Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018 you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you (Article 15)
- **Rectify** inaccurate data (Article 16)
- **Erasure** — request deletion ("right to be forgotten") (Article 17)
- **Restrict** processing (Article 18)
- **Data portability** (Article 20)
- **Object** to processing (Article 21)
- **Withdraw consent at any time** (Article 7(3)) — this is the key one for testimonials

**To withdraw consent or exercise any other right:** email `privacy@theenglishhub.app` or `dpo@theenglishhub.app`. We will:

- Remove your testimonial from our website within **14 days**
- Cease running any paid advertising featuring you within **30 days** (Meta/Google ad review cycles)
- Retain a copy of the original consent form for 2 years solely for audit / defence
- Not use new withdrawal as grounds to treat you differently in any commercial relationship

If you are unhappy with our handling of your personal data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office: ico.org.uk · 0303 123 1113.
