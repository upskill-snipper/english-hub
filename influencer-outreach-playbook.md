## 1. INFLUENCER TARGET LIST

### Tier Definitions
- **Nano:** 1K-5K followers (highest engagement, easiest to land)
- **Micro:** 5K-50K followers (sweet spot for ROI)
- **Mid:** 50K-200K followers (broader reach, still accessible)
- **Macro:** 200K+ followers (aspirational, lower response rate)

Priority: Nano and Micro influencers first. They convert better, respond faster, and are more willing to try a new platform.

### Follow-Up Rules

1. **Never send more than 3 messages total** (initial + 2 follow-ups)
2. **If they respond negatively at any point**, reply graciously and remove from sequence
3. **If they open the email but don't reply** (trackable via tools below), the Day 7 follow-up becomes more important
4. **If they respond positively**, immediately move to the Onboarding Sequence (below)

### Step-by-Step Setup: Instantly.ai + Hunter.io + Notion CRM

**This setup handles email outreach at scale for ~$65/month total.**

#### Step 1: Set Up Your Sending Infrastructure (Day 1)

1. **Purchase a secondary domain** for outreach (e.g., `theenglishhub.co` if your main domain is `theenglishhub.com`). Never send cold outreach from your primary domain.
2. **Create 2-3 email accounts** on this domain (e.g., partnerships@, hello@, [yourname]@).
3. **Sign up for Instantly.ai** (Growth plan, $30/month).
4. **Connect your email accounts** to Instantly.
5. **Enable email warmup** in Instantly — let it run for 14 days before sending any outreach. This builds sender reputation.

#### Step 2: Build Your Contact List (Day 1-7, ongoing)

1. **Create a master spreadsheet** with columns: Name, Handle, Platform, Email, Category (A-E), Follower Count, Notes, Status.
2. **For each influencer on your target list:**
   - Check their social media bio for email addresses (many creators list a business email)
   - If no email visible, use **Hunter.io**: enter their website domain (e.g., mrbruff.com) to find associated emails
   - For creators without websites, try Hunter.io's "Email Finder" with their name + likely domain
   - Check their YouTube "About" page (many list business inquiry emails)
   - Check their Linktree or similar link-in-bio tools
3. **Separate into two lists:** Email contacts (for Instantly) and DM-only contacts (for manual outreach)

### A/B Testing Plan

**Test 1: Subject Lines (Email Outreach)**

| Variant | Subject Line | Test Period | Audience |
|---------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| A (Control) | "A resource your GCSE English students might actually use, [FIRST NAME]" | Week 1-2 | Teachers — Wave 1 |
| B (Curiosity) | "Quick question about your [PLATFORM] content, [FIRST NAME]" | Week 1-2 | Teachers — Wave 2 |
| C (Direct) | "20% recurring commission — GCSE English affiliate programme" | Week 3-4 | YouTubers |
| D (Value-led) | "Free GCSE English platform for your audience, [FIRST NAME]" | Week 3-4 | YouTubers |

**Metric:** Open rate. Winner used for all subsequent outreach in that category.

---

**Test 2: DM Opening Line**

| Variant | Opening | Test Period |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| A (Compliment-first) | "Genuinely love your [SPECIFIC CONTENT]..." | Week 1-2, odd-numbered contacts |
| B (Question-first) | "Quick question — do your followers ever ask you for GCSE English revision tools?" | Week 1-2, even-numbered contacts |

**Metric:** Reply rate. Split contacts alternately.

---

**Test 3: CTA Style**

| Variant | Call to Action | Test Period |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| A (Low commitment) | "Would you be up for a free account to try it out?" | Weeks 1-3 |
| B (Specific action) | "Can I send you a 2-minute demo video?" | Weeks 4-6 |
| C (Social proof) | "Want to join [X] other creators already earning with us?" | Weeks 7+ (once you have partners) |

**Metric:** Response rate and conversion to onboarded partner.

---

**Test 4: Follow-Up Timing**

| Variant | Follow-Up 1 | Follow-Up 2 | Test Period |
|---------|------------|------------|-------------|
| A (Standard) | Day 3 | Day 7 | Weeks 1-4 |
| B (Compressed) | Day 2 | Day 5 | Weeks 5-8 |

**Metric:** Overall response rate across the full sequence.

---

### A/B Testing Rules

1. **Minimum sample size:** 30 contacts per variant before drawing conclusions
2. **Only test one variable at a time** (don't change subject line AND CTA simultaneously)
3. **Track in your Notion CRM** by adding a "Test Variant" property (Select: A, B, C, D)
4. **Review results fortnightly** and implement winners
5. **Document everything** — what you tested, sample size, result, date implemented
