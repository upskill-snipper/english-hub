# Pricing Sheet — The English Hub

A single, plain-English pricing sheet. This is the document you send to a school once they have seen the demo and asked how much it costs. It is deliberately short.

**Rule:** All prices shown here are for the UK market in pounds sterling, excluding VAT, for schools only. Do not use this sheet for tutors, consumers, or non-UK customers — there is a separate price list for those.

---

## Standard pricing — the headline

The English Hub is priced per pupil per year. A pupil is any pupil in the year groups you include in your licence.

| Year groups covered | Standard price per pupil per year |
|---|---|
| KS3 only (Years 7–9) | £12 |
| KS4 only (Years 10–11) | £15 |
| KS3 and KS4 combined | £22 |
| Whole secondary (Years 7–13) | £26 |

All prices include:

- Unlimited teacher accounts
- Every module and feature in the platform
- Termly feature updates
- UK-based customer support, Monday to Friday
- Integration with Google, Microsoft and most school SSO providers
- Data processing agreement (DPA) and UK data hosting
- Onboarding session for the department

There are no setup fees, no per-teacher fees, and no hidden add-ons.

## Founding Schools pricing — for the first ten only

Founding Schools receive **50 percent off** their Year 1 licence. That's it — no tricks, no reduced feature set, no cap on users.

| Year groups covered | Standard Y1 | Founding School Y1 (50% off) |
|---|---|---|
| KS3 only (Years 7–9) | £12 / pupil | **£6 / pupil** |
| KS4 only (Years 10–11) | £15 / pupil | **£7.50 / pupil** |
| KS3 and KS4 combined | £22 / pupil | **£11 / pupil** |
| Whole secondary (Years 7–13) | £26 / pupil | **£13 / pupil** |

In addition, Founding Schools receive:

- A **Year 2 renewal cap**: the price in Year 2 cannot rise by more than 10 percent on the standard rate — locked for the life of the reference relationship.
- Direct line to the founder (private Slack or WhatsApp channel, monthly call).
- Priority feature requests (up to three guaranteed ships per year).
- Co-marketing: case study, joint webinar, logo, press introductions.

See `01-positioning/programme-overview.md` for full terms.

## Worked examples

### Example 1 — Small secondary (600 pupils total, KS3+KS4 only, 800 pupils in relevant years)

- Standard Year 1: 800 × £22 = **£17,600**
- Founding School Year 1: 800 × £11 = **£8,800**
- Saving: **£8,800** in Year 1

### Example 2 — Medium secondary (1,200 pupils KS3+KS4)

- Standard Year 1: 1,200 × £22 = **£26,400**
- Founding School Year 1: 1,200 × £11 = **£13,200**
- Saving: **£13,200** in Year 1

### Example 3 — Large secondary with sixth form (1,800 pupils KS3+KS4+KS5)

- Standard Year 1: 1,800 × £26 = **£46,800**
- Founding School Year 1: 1,800 × £13 = **£23,400**
- Saving: **£23,400** in Year 1

### Example 4 — MAT of 4 schools, 800 pupils each (KS3+KS4)

- Standard Year 1: 4 × 800 × £22 = **£70,400**
- Founding School Year 1 (assuming each school takes a slot): 4 × 800 × £11 = **£35,200**
- Additional MAT volume discount: 10% on top of Founding Schools pricing for 4+ schools on a single agreement
- Final Founding School MAT price: **£31,680**

Note: MATs that take multiple Founding Schools slots at the same time count against the total 10-slot pool. A MAT with 4 schools takes 4 of the 10 Founding Schools slots.

## Pilot pricing — free

A 4-week pilot for any school is **free**. Specifically:

- Free: the licence for the pilot duration
- Free: 90 minutes of founder-led onboarding
- Free: a mid-pilot check-in and an end-of-pilot review
- Free: the summary report of pilot results

The pilot is restricted to a single class of up to 30 pupils. See `06-close/pilot-agreement-template.md` for the short agreement.

After the pilot, there is no obligation to sign. Either you buy the platform or we shake hands and go our separate ways.

## Payment terms

- **Invoice cadence:** Annual, in advance.
- **Payment terms:** 30 days net.
- **Payment method:** BACS (preferred), purchase order accepted. We also accept credit card for schools that prefer it — no processing fee charged.
- **Cancellation:** Year 1 is a 12-month commitment. After Year 1, 30 days' notice to non-renew.
- **Price lock:** Once signed, the Year 1 price is locked. Any price increases apply only to renewal.
- **VAT:** All prices exclude VAT. VAT is charged at the prevailing UK rate (currently 20 percent). Schools exempt from VAT should provide a certificate at the point of signing.

## What is explicitly not included

To avoid nasty surprises later, these are not in the base licence and are not offered at the moment:

- Custom content development (your own schemes of work built for you)
- Bespoke integrations with proprietary school MIS systems
- On-site full-day training beyond the 90-minute onboarding
- White-labelling or private-branded versions
- Weekend or out-of-hours customer support

If any of these are important to a school, flag them in the commercial conversation. Do not promise them without checking with the founder first.

## When to deviate from this pricing

Do not deviate without thinking hard. The reasons you might:

1. **MAT-wide sale (4+ schools):** Add a 10 percent volume discount on top of Founding Schools pricing, as per Example 4.
2. **Multi-year commitment:** If a Founding School wants to commit to 3 years, offer a further 5 percent discount on Years 2 and 3, provided the 10 percent uplift cap remains.
3. **Early payment:** 2 percent discount for annual payment in advance 60+ days early. Rare but occasionally useful.
4. **Reference case study + video:** 5 percent discount for a school that commits to a filmed case study.

Never deviate on:

- Year 1 base Founding Schools discount (always exactly 50 percent).
- The 10-slot cap.
- Free pilot terms.

## What to do with this sheet

1. Do not email this sheet before the demo. Schools pattern-match to "sales collateral" and tune out.
2. After the demo, send a customised one-page version that includes only the table relevant to them and a worked example using their actual pupil numbers.
3. Include a clear call to action: "To reserve a Founding Schools slot, sign the pilot agreement and pick a start date."
4. Walk the school through the numbers verbally at least once. Never let price land cold in an email.
