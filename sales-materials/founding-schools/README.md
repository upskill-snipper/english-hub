# Founding Schools Programme — Sales & Pitch Pack

This pack contains everything the founder needs to sell The English Hub to the first ten UK secondary schools. These schools are the **Founding Schools** — the initial reference customers whose logos, quotes and case studies will underpin all future growth.

## Why this pack exists

- The first 10 schools are disproportionately important. They provide social proof, product feedback, and case studies.
- The founder is the best (and currently only) salesperson for this stage.
- This pack removes every excuse to not send the email, make the call, or take the meeting.

## How to use this pack

Work through the folders in order on any given deal:

| Folder | When to use | What's inside |
|---|---|---|
| `01-positioning` | Before any outreach, when refining the message | Programme overview, key messages, UVP vs competitors |
| `02-pitch-deck` | For scheduled pitch meetings (30–60 mins) | Deck outline, speaker notes, demo script |
| `03-outreach` | To generate top-of-funnel meetings | Cold email sequences, LinkedIn templates, phone script, referral ask |
| `04-meetings` | During discovery and demo calls | Discovery script, demo agenda, objection handling, MEDDPICC questions |
| `05-commercials` | When commercial conversations start | Pricing sheet, ROI calculator, contract summary, mutual action plan |
| `06-close` | To move deals over the line | Proposal, pilot agreement, case study, reference letter |
| `07-tracking` | Every day | Pipeline tracker CSV, activity log CSV |

## The Founding Schools offer in one sentence

> Become one of our first ten partner schools, get 50 percent off your first year, a direct line to the founder, priority on feature requests, and co-marketing — in exchange for being a reference site.

## The rules of engagement

1. **No gatekeeping.** Go directly to the Head of English or MAT curriculum lead. Don't wait for procurement to surface you.
2. **Speed over polish.** A one-line personalised email beats a ten-paragraph template.
3. **Offer the pilot.** Four weeks free, no credit card, no integrations needed.
4. **Write it up.** Every call goes into the activity log (`07-tracking/activity-log-template.csv`) within 24 hours.
5. **Ask for the close.** Every meeting ends with a specific next step and a date.
6. **Ask for a reference.** Every founding school signs the reference commitment letter (`06-close/reference-commitment-letter.md`) at contract signature.

## Target schools (ICP)

- UK secondary schools (state or independent), KS3 and KS4 English departments
- Between 600 and 1,800 students
- English department of 6 to 15 teachers
- Already using at least one edtech tool (i.e. budget exists, procurement pathway understood)
- MATs with 3 to 20 schools are especially high value — one sale delivers multiple logos

## Weekly cadence for the founder

- **Monday:** Plan the week. Pick the 5 schools to contact, pull from the pipeline tracker.
- **Tuesday–Thursday:** Outbound — 20 cold emails, 5 LinkedIn messages, 5 phone calls, 2 meetings a day.
- **Friday:** Write up notes, send follow-ups, update the pipeline tracker, ship case study content from the week's meetings.

## Success metrics

- **Weeks 1–4:** 40 meetings booked, 10 pilots started
- **Weeks 5–12:** 10 Founding Schools signed, 6 case studies drafted
- **Post-programme:** 10 reference logos on the website, 6 published case studies, 3 schools willing to take a reference call

## Placeholders used throughout

- `[SCHOOL NAME]` — the target school
- `[HEAD OF ENGLISH]` — the decision-maker contact
- `[FIRST NAME]` — first-name salutation
- `[FOUNDER NAME]` — the founder's name
- `[CALENDAR LINK]` — the meeting scheduler
- `[DEMO URL]` — product demo link
- `[PRICING]` — insert the current price from the pricing sheet
- `[DATE]` — relevant date
- `[CASE STUDY LINK]` — once available, link a real case study

Replace these before sending.
