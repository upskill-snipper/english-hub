# Proposal Template — Founding Schools Programme

A written proposal template customisable per school. This is sent after a successful demo meeting, typically the day after, alongside the contract and DPA. It is the document that gets forwarded to the head teacher and the business manager for approval.

**Length target:** 4–6 pages when formatted. No longer.
**Tone:** Professional but warm. This isn't a corporate procurement doc. It's a letter from a former teacher to a Head of English.
**Format:** Send as a clean PDF with a branded cover page.

### The pilot

We are offering a free 4-week pilot before any commercial commitment. The terms are simple:

- **Length:** 4 weeks.
- **Scope:** One class of up to 30 pupils, selected by [SCHOOL NAME].
- **Cost:** Free. No credit card required. No purchase order needed.
- **Onboarding:** A 90-minute session with the nominated teacher, delivered by [FOUNDER NAME] personally.
- **Mid-pilot check-in:** A 20-minute call at the 2-week mark to catch any issues early.

At the end of the pilot, you have three options:

1. **Proceed to a Founding Schools contract** and the pilot class transitions into the Year 1 licence.
2. **Extend the pilot** for a further 2 weeks if you want more data before committing.
3. **Walk away** with no obligation, and we wish you well.

### Data protection and security

- **GDPR:** Fully compliant. Data Processing Agreement ready to sign alongside the main contract.
- **Hosting:** All pupil data hosted in the UK.
- **AI data use:** Pupil data is never used to train AI models.
- **Teacher in the loop:** No AI-generated feedback reaches a pupil without a teacher's approval click.
- **Single sign-on:** Supports Google Workspace and Microsoft 365. Pupils do not create new passwords.
- **Data export on exit:** Full data export within 30 days of contract end; all copies deleted within 60 days.

Full data protection documentation is available to your DPO on request.

## How to customise this template

1. Replace every bracketed field with the specific information for the school.
2. Remove any sections that don't apply — e.g. if the school has no MAT, remove MAT references.
3. Add a brief paragraph in the "Letter from the founder" section that references one specific thing from the demo meeting.
4. Update the timeline dates to reflect the MAP you've agreed.
5. Generate a PDF and email it with a short covering email that restates the next step.

## What to attach to the proposal email

- This proposal (PDF)
- The pilot agreement (Word doc, so they can edit)
- The pricing sheet (PDF, for reference)
- The one-page Founding Schools overview (PDF)
- A demo recording link, if permission was given

## What not to do

- Do not send the proposal without a covering email.
- Do not send the proposal as a Word doc — it looks unfinished.
- Do not attach everything at once. Two attachments in the initial email is enough. Offer the rest on request.
- Do not include pricing in the email body — keep it in the proposal itself. It gives the school a reason to open the document.
