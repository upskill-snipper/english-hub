# Tier 1 — Top 50 Priority Schools

The top 50 British international schools ranked by deal probability times deal size. These are where we should invest research time, personalised outreach, and travel.

**Ranking factors:**
1. **Size** — student numbers and English department size.
2. **Budget** — fee levels, parent body, known resource spend.
3. **Prestige / replication effect** — winning a flagship unlocks word-of-mouth.
4. **Group leverage** — winning the school can open the group.
5. **Curriculum match** — pure IGCSE focus scores higher than IB-only.
6. **Accessibility** — openness to third-party content, existing tech maturity.

All contact fields should be refreshed before outreach. Heads of English rotate 3-5 yearly.

## Action Plan for Tier 1

1. **Research week (Week 1):** for each of 50 schools, find: current Head of School, Head of Secondary, Head of English, English department size, most recent public exam results, IGCSE exam board.
2. **Personalisation (Week 2):** draft a personalised email referencing a specific fact (recent inspection, exam board, new building, alumni in UK universities).
3. **Outbound wave 1 (Week 3-4):** send all 50 on a staggered schedule — max 10 per day to stay deliverable.
4. **Follow-up (Week 4-6):** 5-touch sequence from `04-outreach/follow-up-sequence.md`.
5. **Target outcomes:**
   - 50 sent → 12 responses → 8 exploratory calls → 4 demos → 2 pilots → 1 closed paid contract.
   - Aim for the cycle to run in 90 days.
6. **Group escalation (parallel):** for any GEMS / Nord Anglia / Dulwich / Cognita / Inspired school that responds, immediately request intro to group-level procurement.

## Notes on Research Tools

- **Cambridge International Find a School:** cambridgeinternational.org
- **COBIS Find a School:** cobis.org.uk
- **BSME Find a School:** bsme.org.uk/find-a-school
- **FOBISIA Member Schools:** fobisia.org/our-members
- **International Schools Database:** international-schools-database.com
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** filter by industry (Primary/Secondary), title (Head of English, English Teacher, Head of Secondary), geography.
- **Wayback Machine:** check old versions of school websites to see previous staff lists.
- **ISC Research:** subscription required but the definitive source.
