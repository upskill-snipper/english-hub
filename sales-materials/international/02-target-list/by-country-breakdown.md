# Target Schools by Country

The 200-school target list broken down by country, for running country-by-country campaigns. Each country section also notes campaign timing, local context, and key tactical notes.

Refer to `top-200-schools.md` for complete profiles; this file is a quick-reference index.

## Middle East

### United Arab Emirates — 25 schools

**Dubai (15):** GEMS Wellington International, GEMS Wellington Academy Silicon Oasis, GEMS Wellington Academy Al Khail, GEMS Cambridge International, GEMS Winchester School, GEMS Founders School, GEMS Royal Dubai School, Dubai College, Jumeirah College (GEMS), Nord Anglia International School Dubai, Dulwich College Dubai, Kings' School Dubai, Repton School Dubai, Brighton College Dubai, Horizon International School Dubai.

**Abu Dhabi + others (10):** British School Al Khubairat, Cranleigh Abu Dhabi, Brighton College Abu Dhabi, Brighton College Al Ain, Repton School Abu Dhabi, Al Yasmina Academy, Al Bateen Academy, Sharjah English School, The British International School Ajman, RAK Academy.

**Campaign notes:**
- Fiscal year typically starts in September; budget sign-off is January-April.
- KHDA (Dubai) and ADEK (Abu Dhabi) regulate curriculum approvals.
- **Best time to pitch:** October-February.
- **Best event:** BETT Middle East (October/November, Dubai).
- **Leverage:** GEMS group = 9 schools in one conversation.

### Qatar — 8 schools
Doha British School (Ain Khaled), Doha British School Al Wakra, Compass International School Doha, Park House English School, Sherborne Qatar, Nord Anglia Al Khor, Newton British School, Doha Academy.

**Campaign notes:**
- Smaller market but high fees and tight-knit community.
- **Best time to pitch:** September-November for Jan-Apr decisions.
- **Leverage:** Doha British School owns 3 campuses.

### Saudi Arabia — 8 schools

## Campaign Sequencing Recommendations

| Quarter | Region focus | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Middle East (GCC) | Budget cycle sign-off happens Jan-Apr; BSME Annual in Jan-Feb. |
| Q2 | UK + Europe (Spain, Portugal, Italy, NL, DE) | Nabss and COBIS Annual Conference in May. |
| Q3 | Asia (Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, HK) | Academic year starts Aug-Sep; FOBISIA Teachers Conference Oct-Nov. |
| Q4 | Americas + Africa | Quieter markets that benefit from focused attention; end-of-year pipeline push. |
