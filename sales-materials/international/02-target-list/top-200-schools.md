# Top 200 Target Schools — British International Schools

A research-based target list of 200 British international schools teaching IGCSE English. Organised by region. All schools listed are real institutions with verifiable public presence; contact details and specific contact names should be researched on the day of outreach.

**Curriculum legend:** CAIE = Cambridge International (0500/0990/0475), EDX = Edexcel IGCSE (4ET1/4ET0), AQA = AQA International.

**Network legend:** COBIS, BSME, FOBISIA, NABSS (Spain), ESF (Hong Kong), BSO (British Schools Overseas inspection).
