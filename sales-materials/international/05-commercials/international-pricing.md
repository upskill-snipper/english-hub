# International Pricing — Single-School Deals

Pricing for individual British international schools buying The English Hub at the department level. Group pricing is covered separately in `school-group-framework-pricing.md`.

## Core principle

**Per student, per year, annual contract.** Predictable, budgetable, matches how international schools book other resources (textbooks, exam fees, licensing).

## Contract terms

- **Term length:** 12 months standard, 24 months with 5% discount, 36 months with 10% discount.
- **Payment:** annual in advance is default. Semi-annual available with 3% uplift.
- **Renewal:** auto-renew on 60 days' notice unless cancelled.
- **Price protection:** list price locked for contract term; annual CPI adjustment possible on 24/36 month deals (capped at 5% per year).
- **Billing entity:** we can bill the school directly, a group entity, or a nominated billing agent.
- **Cancellation:** for convenience cancellation only at term end (pro-rata refund not standard).
- **Data processing:** GDPR / UK GDPR DPA in place; localised DPAs available for UAE, KSA, Singapore, China as needed.

## Tax considerations

| Region | Tax approach | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **UK** | Standard UK VAT (20%) if selling from UK entity | Not normally applicable to overseas school customers |
| **EU (B2B)** | Reverse charge — school accounts for VAT in their country | Requires school VAT number on the invoice |
| **EU (B2C, private)** | VAT MOSS scheme; charge school's country's VAT rate | Only applies if school is not VAT-registered |
| **UAE** | 5% VAT if UAE entity bills UAE school | Educational services may be zero-rated or exempt — check per emirate and category |
| **Saudi Arabia** | 15% VAT if KSA entity bills KSA school | ZATCA rules; may be zero-rated for licensed educational services |
| **Qatar, Bahrain** | 5-10% VAT | Region-specific; check locally |
| **Singapore** | GST (9% currently) if SG-registered seller | International sales from outside SG often exempt |
| **India** | GST 18% (educational services sometimes exempt) | Complex; legal review required for India sales |
| **China** | VAT 6% on digital services | Cross-border complex; partner route may be simpler |
| **Brazil** | Multiple taxes including PIS, COFINS, ISS | Very complex; use local partner or representative |
| **Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa** | VAT 14-16% varies | Local representative recommended for scale |
| **All others** | Check locally; assume reverse charge or zero-rated for digital exports | |

**Invoicing practical note:** we issue invoices in USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SGD by default. Other currencies via special request. Invoices include itemised per-student pricing, contract term, tax treatment and payment terms (NET 30 standard, NET 60 for large groups or state-linked schools).

## How to pitch pricing on a call

1. **Confirm cohort size first.** "How many students do you have in Years 7-13 studying English?" Never price before knowing the number.
2. **Offer the band, not the exact number.** "For a school your size, schools typically land in the {tier} tier at about {price} per student per year."
3. **Anchor against alternatives.** "That's less than a textbook per student per year, and it covers every IGCSE paper and every set text."
4. **Show multi-year value.** "On a 3-year contract you'd lock in 10% below list, which gives your bursar predictability across the planning cycle."
5. **Close on next step, not price.** "If the shape works, I'll send a formal proposal with exact numbers in your currency. Should we target a start date at the beginning of next term?"

## Pricing objections and responses

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "That's too expensive per student." | "Let's compare it to the current cost of teacher prep time on internally-built materials. If we save a teacher 3 hours a week and their hourly cost is {region-specific number}, we pay for ourselves in a month." |
| "Other providers charge less." | "The others are either mainland UK GCSE-focused or not examiner-written. If another provider is genuinely cheaper AND covers Cambridge and Edexcel IGCSE at spec level with examiner-written content, I'd love to know about it." |
| "We can't budget that this year." | "I understand. Two options: start from term 2 on a 6-month licence at pro-rata, or lock in a price today for a start date in your next budget year." |
| "Why per student and not per school?" | "Per student keeps it honest — a 2,500-pupil school gets more value than a 300-pupil school, so it scales fairly. It also makes the budget line obvious at renewal — no surprises." |
| "Can we just have teacher accounts without student accounts?" | "We offer a teacher-only tier on request for smaller schools, priced at {teacher-only rate}. You lose the student-facing tools but keep full teacher resources." |
