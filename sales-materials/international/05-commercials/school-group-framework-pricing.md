# School Group Framework Pricing

Commercial structure for multi-school group deals (Nord Anglia, GEMS, Cognita, Dulwich International, AISL Harrow, Wellington International, Inspired, Aldar, Globeducate, Braeburn, etc.).

The pricing on this page is the starting point for a group negotiation. Final commercial terms will always be negotiated deal-by-deal.

## Framework structure

A group framework has three tiers:

1. **Master Services Agreement (MSA)** — contractual umbrella, signed at group HQ level, covering all schools in the group.
2. **Regional or per-school work orders** — activate the MSA for specific schools or regions.
3. **Annual subscription payment** — per-student annual invoicing, regional billing possible.

This structure lets a group commit at the HQ level without every school having to sign a separate contract, while still giving the group visibility into which schools are active.

## Contract and commercial terms for groups

- **Term length:** 36 months standard. Groups generally prefer 3+ years for predictability.
- **Minimum commitment:** group agrees a minimum of N schools active in Year 1, N+ in Year 2, N++ in Year 3.
- **Activation model:** schools opt in under the MSA via work order, or group HQ mandates activation.
- **Termination:** either party may terminate for convenience at term end with 90 days' notice; termination for cause (material breach) on 30 days' notice + cure period.
- **Price protection:** rates locked for full term; CPI uplift capped at 4%.
- **Change of control:** if the group is acquired or restructured, the MSA survives.
- **Data processing:** DPA included; data residency addendums available per region.
- **IP:** we retain IP on our content; group gets a licence for the contract term.
- **Co-marketing:** option for joint press release, joint case study, logo use — negotiated per deal.
- **Exclusivity:** we do not offer category exclusivity (we can still sell to other groups). We can offer regional exclusivity in exceptional cases for premium pricing.

## Payment and billing

- **Invoice cadence:** annual in advance standard; quarterly or monthly available for larger groups with finance teams that prefer it.
- **Billing entity:** group HQ OR regional entity OR per-school — group's choice.
- **Currency:** USD / GBP / EUR default; regional currency available.
- **Payment terms:** NET 45 for groups (longer than single-school NET 30).
- **Late payment:** 1.5% per month after NET 45, subject to local law.
- **FX risk:** if billing in a non-base currency, rate is fixed at contract signing and reviewed annually.

## Pilot programme

Every group deal starts with a pilot of 2-5 schools for one term. Pilot terms:

- **Duration:** 12 weeks (one academic term)
- **Scope:** 2-5 schools nominated by the group
- **Cost:** pilot cost credited against the first year of the framework agreement if it proceeds
- **Success criteria:** defined up front with the group. Typical metrics:
  - Teacher NPS ≥ +30
  - Weekly active usage ≥ 60% of teachers
  - Content usage ≥ 10 resources per teacher per month
  - Head of English endorsement for framework
- **Deliverables:** joint pilot report, case study, group-level briefing

## Negotiation guardrails

- **Floor price per student:** USD 5 at the largest group tier. Below this, walk.
- **Minimum first-year committed revenue:** USD 30,000 to qualify as a "framework" — smaller groups get standard single-school pricing.
- **Setup and onboarding fee:** absorbed at Group C+; charged at Group A-B.
- **Custom content:** not included; always priced separately.
- **Exclusive-region commitments:** only with Group E/F pricing and a 5-year minimum term.
