# International Schools Sales Pack — The English Hub

A complete sales enablement pack targeting British international schools worldwide delivering IGCSE English Language and Literature.

## Why This Matters

There are approximately 9,000 English-medium international schools globally, with over 6,000 delivering a British curriculum. The market grew from roughly 2,500 schools in 2000 to over 13,000 English-medium schools today (ISC Research), and British curriculum remains the dominant export model, especially for IGCSE (Cambridge 0500/0990 and Edexcel 4ET1).

For The English Hub, this is a **high-ticket, high-retention** segment:
- Fee-paying private schools with real procurement budgets (no DfE-style austerity).
- Parents and boards who value British-quality resources.
- Examiner-written content is a genuine differentiator (most have local staff who didn't train in the UK).
- One decision at a group level (Nord Anglia, GEMS, Cognita, Dulwich, Harrow) can land 40-100 schools at once.
- USD/EUR/AED/SGD pricing, typically 2-3x UK GCSE rates.

## How to Use

1. **Read first:** `01-market-research/market-overview.md` and `03-positioning/why-english-hub-for-international-schools.md`.
2. **Pick a campaign:** use `02-target-list/tier-1-top-priority-50.md` for focused outreach or `02-target-list/by-country-breakdown.md` for regional campaigns.
3. **Group deals first:** always prioritise `02-target-list/school-groups-chains.md` — a single conversation at Nord Anglia HQ is worth 80 individual conversations.
4. **Send outreach:** use `04-outreach/` templates, personalise with school-specific hooks, run `follow-up-sequence.md`.
5. **Price the deal:** `05-commercials/international-pricing.md` for single schools, `school-group-framework-pricing.md` for chains.
6. **Meet them in person:** `06-events/conferences-to-attend.md` — COBIS Annual and BETT Middle East are must-attend.

## Sequencing

**Quarter 1:** Tier 1 cold outreach + COBIS/BSME contact. Target: 10 demos, 3 pilot schools.
**Quarter 2:** Group HQ conversations (Nord Anglia, GEMS, Cognita). Attend BETT Middle East.
**Quarter 3:** Regional waves — Middle East then Asia then Europe. Attend COBIS Annual Conference.
**Quarter 4:** Close tier 1 pilots, convert to group frameworks, expand into tier 2.
