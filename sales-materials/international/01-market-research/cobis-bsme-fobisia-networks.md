# COBIS, BSME, FOBISIA and Related Networks

The three most important British international school associations are COBIS (global), BSME (Middle East), and FOBISIA (Asia). Joining them as a supporting associate is the single highest-leverage move we can make in this segment.

## COBIS — Council of British International Schools

- **HQ:** London, UK.
- **Leadership:** CEO Colin Bell. Board drawn from member school heads and governors.
- **Membership:** approximately 331 member schools across 80+ countries (figure continues to grow).
- **Affiliate / supporting members:** around 193 supporting associate organisations — publishers, tech providers, training firms, recruitment agencies. This is the tier we would join.
- **Website:** cobis.org.uk

### What membership gets us
- Listed in the COBIS supporting associates directory (warm introduction channel).
- Access to member school contact lists via shared communications.
- Ability to sponsor / exhibit at the COBIS Annual Conference (May, London) — the flagship event, 900+ attendees.
- Eligibility to run webinars and CPD sessions for COBIS members.
- Discounted access to COBIS CPD, Inclusion, and regional events.
- Rights to use COBIS branding in marketing as a supporting associate.

### How to access the member list
1. Join as a supporting associate (annual fee typically in the low £k range — negotiate based on committed sponsorship).
2. Once listed, request the member directory through the COBIS Supporting Associate liaison.
3. Alternatively, the public website has a searchable "Find a School" tool — we can scrape this legally for the first pass target list.

### Key COBIS events
- **COBIS Annual Conference** (London, May): 900+ delegates, 300+ schools, 3 days. Exhibition booths from £3-8k depending on size and tier.
- **COBIS Student Conference** (year round, various regions).
- **Bursars and Business Managers Conference** (Oct, London).
- **Marketing, Development and Admissions Conference** (Oct, London).
- **Conference for School Leaders in Training** (multiple regions).

## BSME — British Schools in the Middle East

- **HQ:** UAE.
- **Founded:** 1982.
- **Membership:** ~90 member schools across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Egypt, Jordan, Lebanon.
- **Website:** bsme.org.uk

### What membership gets us
- "Find A School" directory listing for schools; "School Members" and "Supporting Associates" directories.
- Right to exhibit at BSME Annual Conference.
- Regional working groups (secondary, primary, SEND, leadership).
- Networking dinners at BSME events (relationship-dense region).
- Branding rights.

### How to access the member list
- BSME website publishes a complete member school list at bsme.org.uk/find-a-school — freely accessible.
- For contact information at a member school, we need to either attend the annual conference or introduce via our own warm network.

### Key BSME events
- **BSME Annual Conference** (Jan-Feb, rotating GCC city): ~300 delegates. Top event to meet GCC heads.
- **BSME Leadership Conference** (regional heads focused).
- **BSME SEND Conference**.
- **BSME Student Leadership Events**.

## FOBISIA — Federation of British International Schools in Asia

- **HQ:** Kuala Lumpur (regional secretariat); board of member heads.
- **Founded:** 1988.
- **Membership:** 107 member schools across 20 countries and regions in Asia-Pacific (Brunei, China, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Japan, Kazakhstan, Laos, Malaysia, Mongolia, Myanmar, Nepal, Philippines, Singapore, South Korea, Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam).
- **Affiliate members:** education-sector suppliers, inc. iSAMS, Lego Education, recruiters.
- **Website:** fobisia.org

### What membership gets us
- Listed in FOBISIA affiliate directory.
- Right to exhibit at FOBISIA conferences (Heads, Business Managers, Teachers, ICT, SEN).
- Access to Head of English working groups across Asia.
- Regional PR and comms channels.

### How to access the member list
- FOBISIA website publishes the member directory at fobisia.org/our-members/member-school.
- Direct approach via Head of English preferred; avoid mass marketing to Asia-based schools as that damages relationships fast.

### Key FOBISIA events
- **FOBISIA Heads' Conference** (March).
- **FOBISIA Business Managers' Conference**.
- **FOBISIA Teachers' Conference** (Oct-Nov).
- **FOBISIA ICT and Leadership events**.
- **Annual FOBISIA Games** (sports, but heads attend).

## Other Networks Worth Knowing

| Network | Region | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **NABSS** | Spain | National Association of British Schools in Spain. Annual conference, shared marketing bodies. |
| **BSO** | Global | British Schools Overseas inspection regime — the UK government accreditation route. Not a trade body but inspection status signals quality. |
| **AoBSO** | Global | Association of British Schools Overseas — smaller association; overlaps with COBIS. |
| **ECIS** | Europe | Educational Collaborative for International Schools — broader than BC-only but includes many. |
| **AAIE** | Americas | Association for the Advancement of International Education — mostly US-curriculum but BC schools attend. |
| **CIS** | Global | Council of International Schools — accreditation body including BC schools. |
| **ISC Research** | Global | Not a network but the definitive international school data provider. Worth a subscription (~£5-15k/year depending on scope) for lead generation. |
| **WIDA / BSA** | Global | Boarding Schools' Association — includes international British boarding schools like Jerudong, Aiglon, Brighton College Abu Dhabi. |

## Recommended Go-to-Market Sequence

1. **Month 1-2:** Buy supporting associate memberships in COBIS + BSME + FOBISIA simultaneously. Budget: £12-25k total.
2. **Month 2:** Announce the partnerships on LinkedIn, website, and in a joint newsletter. Claim the "official COBIS supporting associate" badge in email sigs.
3. **Month 3:** Commit to exhibiting at BSME Annual (Jan-Feb) and COBIS Annual (May). Budget: £15-35k for both booths + travel.
4. **Month 3-4:** Run a free CPD webinar through each network on "Preparing students for IGCSE 0500 Paper 2 writing" or similar.
5. **Month 4-6:** Follow up every attendee with individual outreach using templates from `04-outreach/`.
6. **Month 6:** Commit to a FOBISIA Heads' Conference exhibit in year 2.
