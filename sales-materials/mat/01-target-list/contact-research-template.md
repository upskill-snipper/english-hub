### Personalisation hook

Write one sentence that proves you've done your homework. This goes in the first line of your cold email.

Examples of good hooks:
- "Loved your talk at ResearchED London on knowledge organisers — the point about assessment lag in English specifically is what we're trying to solve."
- "Congratulations on the Trust's recent Ofsted outcome — the specific callout about cultural capital is what got me to write."
- "Saw you quoted in Schools Week last month on the reading gap post-pandemic — that's the data-loss problem we've been working on for two years."

## Email format guessing (if not publicly listed)

Common MAT email patterns:

- firstname.lastname@trustdomain.org.uk
- finitial.lastname@trustdomain.org.uk
- firstname@trustdomain.org.uk
- flastname@trustdomain.org.uk

Verify with a tool like Hunter.io or neverbounce before sending. Do **not** spray untested addresses — it burns the domain.
