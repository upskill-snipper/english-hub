# MAT Cold Email Templates

**Purpose**: Reach MAT central leads (Director of Education, Director of Curriculum, Head of English at trust level, COO, CEO).

**Principles**:
1. Make the first line about them, not us. No "I hope this finds you well."
2. Short — under 150 words for the opener.
3. One ask. One next step.
4. Plain text. No images. No tracking pixels where possible.
5. Use a real person's name in the signature, not a generic inbox.
6. Research hook in line 1; problem in line 2; specific evidence in line 3; ask in line 4.

**Send times**: Tuesday–Thursday, 07:30 or 17:30 UK time. Avoid Monday and Friday afternoons.

## What not to do in MAT cold outreach

- **Don't mass-send.** MAT central teams talk to each other. One sloppy mass-email burns your reputation with 5 other trusts.
- **Don't send PDFs as your first touch.** It looks like a sales blast.
- **Don't use "circling back" or "just checking in".** Write something worth reading or don't write.
- **Don't promise integrations you haven't tested.** MATs will call you on it in diligence.
- **Don't lead with features.** Lead with a problem they can feel.
- **Don't CC five people at the same trust in the first email.** Pick one, write to them specifically, and ask for the right referral if they're not the right person.
- **Don't use LinkedIn automation.** MAT central teams spot it immediately.
